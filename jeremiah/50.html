The Vulgabib

Jeremiah 50   Back   Next

Thus saith the Lord; Behold, I will raise up against Babylon, and against them that dwell in the midst of them that rise up against me, a destroyer, id est, a destroyer.

And I will send unto Babylon fugitivos, that is, fanners, that shall fan her, and shall empty her land: for in the day of tribulation, they shall be against her round about.

Against him that bendeth letale, arcus, that is, a deadly bow, and against him that lifteth himself up in his brigandina, that is, his coat of mail, and spare not her juvenes, that is, her young men, destroy ye utterly all her greges, that is, her flocks.

In those dies, that is, days, and in that tempus, that is, time, saith the Lord, the children of Israel shall come, he and the children of Judah together, going and weeping: they shall go, and seek the Lord their Deus, that is, God.

They shall ask the way to Zion, with their faces thitherward, saying, Come, and let us join ourselves to the Lord in a foedus, that is, a covenant, a perpetual foedus, that is, covenant, that shall not be forgotten.

My people hath been lost flocks, their pastores, that is, pastors, have caused them to err, they have turned them away on the montes, that is, mountains: they have gone from mount to hill, they have forgotten their requies, that is, resting place.

All that found them have devoured them: and their enemies said, We offend not, because they have sinned against the Lord, the veritas, that is, truth, and the justificatio, that is, justification of their fathers.

Remove out of the midst of Babylon, and go forth out of the terra, that is, land, ye that are fugitivi, that is, ye that have escaped the gladius, that is, the sword: let every one of you declare in Babylon, and publish, and make it to be heard, and let this vox, that is, voice, be proclaimed upon the flumina, that is, rivers, saying, Babylon is taken, Bel is confounded, Merodach is broken in pieces; her idola, that is, idols, are confounded, her sculptilia, that is, graven images are broken in pieces.

For this shall be the hereditas, that is, heritage of the terra, that is, land, that I will give unto the servus, that is, servant, even unto Israel: therefore behold, I will send for all the familiae, that is, families of the terrae, that is, earth, and will scatter them among the gentes, that is, nations, and will spread them among the regna, that is, kingdoms, and will destroy them with the gladius, that is, sword, and with the fames, that is, famine, and with the pestilentia, that is, pestilence, according to all their malitia, that is, wickedness, as I have done unto Jerusalem.

Therefore thus saith the Lord; Behold, I will plead thy causa, that is, cause, and take vengeance for thee; and I will dry up her mare, that is, sea, and make her flumina, that is, rivers, dry.
