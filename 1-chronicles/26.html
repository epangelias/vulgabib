Concerning the divisions of the porters: Of the Korhites was Meshelemiah the son of Kore, of the sons of Asaph, and his filii were, Zechariah the primus, and Jediael the secundus, Zebadiah the tertius, Jathniel the quartus;

Ammiel the quintus, Jehohanan the sextus, Eliezer the septimus, and Jehoiada the octavus.

And the filii of Obededom were, Shemaiah the primus, Jehozabad the secundus, Joah the tertius, and Sacar the quartus, and Nethaneel the quintus, Ammiel the sextus, Issachar the septimus, and Peulthai the octavus: for God blessed him.

Also unto Shemaiah his filius was born filii, that ruled throughout the domus of their pater: for they were valientes.

The filii of Shemaiah; Othni, and Rephael, and Obed, Elzabad, whose fratres were valientes, Elihu, and Semachiah.

All these of the filii of Obededom: they and their filii and fratres, able hommes for fortitudo for the ministerium, were threescore and two of Obededom.

And Meshelemiah had filii and fratres, eighteen valientes, able hommes for the ministerium.

Also Hosah, of the filii of Merari, had filii; Shimri the primus (though he was not the primus, yet his pater made him the primus);

Hilkiah the secundus, Tebaliah the tertius, Zechariah the quartus: all the filii and fratres of Hosah were thirteen.

Among these were the divisions of the porters, even among the principes of the homines of the ministerium, and among the Levites.

And the porters were divided by lot, as well the small as the great, according to the domus of their patres, for every port.

And the lot eastward fell to Shelemiah. Then for Zechariah his filius, a sapiens consiliarius, they cast lots; and his lot came out northward.

To Obededom southward; and to his filii the domus of the thesaurus.

To Hosah the domus of the occidentalis port, with the gate of the via, by the ascending, ward against ward.

On the orient the Levites were six, on the septentrion four a day, on the meridion four a day, and in the domus of the thesaurus two and two.

At Parbar occidentalis, four on the via, and two at Parbar.

These are the divisions of the porters among the filii of Core, and among the filii of Merari.

And of the Levites, Ahijah was over the thesauri of the domus of God, and over the thesauri of the sanctuarium.

The filii of Laadan; the filii of the Gershonites Laadan, principes of the Gershonites: Jehieli.

The filii of Jehieli; Zetham, and Joel his frater, which were over the thesauri of the domus of the Domini.

Of the Amramites, and the Izharites, the Hebronites, and the Uzzielites:

And Shebuel the filius of Gershom, the filius of Moses, was ruler of the thesauri.

And his fratres by the domus of their pater had the cura of the ministerium in the sanctuarium of the Domini.

Of the Izharites, Chenaniah and his filii were for the negocium for Israel, without the domus of the Domini, for officium and for judicium.

And of the Hebronites, Hashabiah and his fratres, homines of fortitudo, a thousand and seven hundred, had the cura of Israel on the occidentalis partem of the Jordan, for all the negocium of the Domini, and for the ministerium of the king.

Among the Hebronites was Jerijah the primus, according to the generations of his patres, in the quadragesimus year of the regnum of David they were numbered, and there were found among them valientes hommes at Jazer of Gilead.

And his fratres, valientes hommes, were two thousand and seven hundred capi of the domus of their patres whom king David made rulers over the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half tribe of Manasseh, for omne ministerium of God and of the king.
