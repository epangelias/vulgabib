And it came to pass, that when Jeremiah had made an end of speaking unto all the people all the words of the Lord their Deus, that the Lord their Deus had sent him to speak, even all these words,

Then spake Azariah the son of Hoshaiah, and Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the proud men, saying unto Jeremiah, Thou speakest falsus, the Lord our Deus hath not sent thee to say, Go not into Aegypt to sojourn there:

But Baruch the filius of Neriah encourageth thee against us, to deliver us into the manus of the Chaldeans, that they might interficere us, and carry us away captivos into Babylon.

So Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the ducem of the exercitus, and all the people, obeyed not the vox of the Lord, to dwell in the terra of Judah.

But Johanan the son of Kareah, and all the ducem of the exercitus, took all the reliquiae of Judah, that were returned from all nations, whither they had been sparsi, to dwell in the terra of Judah;

Even homines, and mulieres, and parvuli, and the filii of the king, and every anima that Nebuzaradan the prince of the cohortis had left with Gedaliah the filius of Ahikam, filius of Shaphan, and Jeremiah the propheta, and Baruch the filius of Neriah.

So they came into the terra of Egypt: for they obeyed not the vox of the Lord: thus came they even to Tahpanhes.

Then came the verbum of the Lord unto Jeremiah in Tahpanhes, saying,

Take great lapides in thy manus, and hide them in the calx in the brickkiln, which is at the os of the rivus Pharaoh, in the sight of the homines of Judah;

And say unto them, Thus saith the Lord of exercitus, the Deus of Israel; Behold, I will send and take Nebuchadrezzar the king of Babylon, my servus, and will set his thronus upon these lapides that I have hid; and he shall spread his tabernaculum over them.

And when he cometh, he shall smite the terra of Aegypt, and deliver such as are for mortem to mortem, and such as are for captivitas to captivitas, and such as are for gladius to gladius.

And I will kindle a ignis in the domus of the deos of Aegypt; and he shall burn them, and carry them away captivos: and he shall array himself with the terra of Aegypt, as a pastor putteth on his vestimentum; and he shall go forth from thence in pace.

He shall break also the statuas of Bethshemesh, that is in the terra of Aegypt; and the domus of the deos of the Aegyptians shall he burn with ignis.
