1 Corinthians 16   Back   Next

1 Now concerning the collection for the saints, as I have given order to the churches of Galatia, even so do ye.

2 Upon the first day of the week let every one of you lay by him in store, as God hath prospered him, that there be no gatherings when I come.

3 And when I come, whomsoever ye shall approve by your letters, them will I send to bring your liberality unto Ierusalem.

4 And if it be meet that I go also, they shall go with me.

5 Now I will come unto you, when I shall pass through Macedonia: for I do pass through Macedonia.

6 And it may be that I will abide, yea, and winter with you, that ye may bring me on my journey whithersoever I go.

7 For I will not see you now by the way; but I trust to tarry a while with you, if the Dominus permit.

8 But I will tarry at Ephesus until Pentecost.

9 For a great and effectual porta is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries.

10 Now if Timotheus come, see that he may be with you without fear: for he worketh the opera Dei, as I also do.

11 Let no man therefore despise him: but conduct him forth in pace, that he may come unto me: for I look for him with the brethren.

12 As touching our brother Apollos, I greatly desired him to come unto you with the brethren: but his voluntas was not at all to come at this time; but he will come when he shall have opportunum.

13 Vigilate, stand fast in the fide, quit you like viri, be fortes.

14 Let all your actions be done in caritas.

15 I beseech you, brethren, (ye know the house of Stephanas, that it is the primafructa of Achaia, and that they have addicted themselves to the ministerium of the saints,)

16 That ye submit yourselves unto such, and to every one that helpeth with us, and laboureth.

17 I am glad of the coming of Stephanas and Fortunatus and Achaicus: for that which was lacking on your part they have supplied.

18 For they have refreshed my spirit and yours: therefore acknowledge ye them that are such.

19 The churches of Asia salute you. Aquila and Priscilla salute you much in Domino, with the ecclesia that is in their domus.

20 All the brethren salute you. Salute one another with a holy osculum.

21 The salutatio of me Paul with mine own manus.

22 If any man love not Dominum Iesus Christus, let him be Anathema Maranatha.

23 The gratia of our Dominus Iesus Christus be with you.

24 My caritas be with you all in Christo Iesu. Amen.
