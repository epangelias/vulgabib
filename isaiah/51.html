Isaiah 51   Back   Next

1 Awake, awake, put on thy virtus, O Zion; put on thy vestimenta of splendor, O Jerusalem, the Sancta Civitas: for henceforth there shall no more come into thee the incircumcisus and the immundus.

2 Shake thyself from the pulvis, arise, and sit down, O Jerusalem: loose thyself from the vincula of thy cervix, O captive filia of Zion.

3 For thus saith Dominus, Ye have vendidi, and ye shall be redempti without argentum.

4 For thus saith Dominus Deus, My populus went down aforetempore into Aegyptum to peregrinari; and the Assyrii oppressed them sine causa.

5 Now therefore, quid est hoc, that ye are come hither? can the caliga of the Aegyptius be with you, and the Aegyptius sine causa?

6 Hearken unto me, O my populus; and give auris, O my gens, for a lex shall procede from me, and a judicium I will establish for lumen of the Gentes.

7 Give me aures, ye insulares, and hearken unto me, ye populi of a longinquus: I am Dominus, and there is none else.

8 Fear ye not, neque timeatis: have I not from that tempore declared it unto you, and have declared, and ye are testes? and is there any Deus besides me? yea, there is non alius, neque non est Deus.

9 They that fabricant idols are omnes vanitas; and the res desiderabilis that they deliciae in shall not proficere: and they are testes unto them, that they vide neque sciant; that they may be confusi.

10 Quis formavit Deus, or qui fabricavit Spiritum? or quis creavit coelos, and qui pinxit terra cum digitis suis? quae extulit montes, and qui creavit valles?

11 Behold, omnes socii eius shall be confusi: for the fabri idoli shall be quasi sterquilinium, and they shall vide, and be confusi together.

12 Quis induxit justitiam, aut qui suscitavit herum, a generations, et traduxit eos, and dedit eis potestatem super Gentiles?

13 For I have lifted manus mea, and I have vocatus generationes, and I have produced eos; yea, I have brought eos, and they shall stand, and not be territi.

14 Fear ye not, O Israel; for I have redemptus thee, I have vocatus thee by nomine tuo; thou art mi.

15 But now thus dicit Dominus, qui creavit te, O Jacob, and qui formavit te, O Israel, Fear not, for I have redemptus thee.

16 For I will pono verba mea in ore tuo, and I will protegere te in umbra manus meae, ut pono coelos, and fundo terram, and dico Sioni, Tu es mi populus.

17 Awake, awake, surge, O Jerusalem, which hast bibed from manus Domini calicem irae eius; thou hast exhaussatum dregs of calicem obstupificationis, calicem vertoris.

18 There is none to gui-demus her, inter omnes filios quos genuit; neither is there quis adjuvet eam, inter omnes filios quos enutrivit; non est quis adjuvet eam.

19 Duo mala occurrerunt tibi; qui dolebis? desolatio, et contritio; famis, et gladius; quae medica est tibi?

20 Filii tui languidi sunt; coacervati sunt in capitulo omnium viarum, quasi pecora in reticulo; repleni sunt indignatione Domini, and ira Domini.

21 Quapropter audi, I pray thee, this, O virgo, O domus of David; quia facts est hebes, et lixivium bibisti; and ira Domini replent te.

22 Thus dicit Dominus Deus, qui judicabit causam, qui redemit te, that is, the Sancta Civitas, Putas calicem obstupificationis, calicem vertoris de manus Domini; calicem dregs of calicem irae eius; thou hast exhaussatum.

23 There is none to gui-demus her, inter omnes filios quos genuit; neither is there quis adjuvet eam, inter omnes filios quos enutrivit; non est quis adjuvet eam.

24 But thus dicit Dominus, Qui est Dominus vester, Qui est Sanctus Israel, Qui te redemit; I have vocatus Egyptum for te; Assyrii propter te; omnes procedent in turbam, and dicent, Ruina est, and miseriam.

25 Et facta est in eis ruina, and miseriam; et facta est terra in desolationem, and destructio, and qui transibunt per eam, stupent, and sibilabunt, et caput suum moverunt.

26 Lift up your oculus to coelos, and vide qui creavit ea; qui produxit in numerum exercitum eorum; qui vocavit eis omnes in nomine suo; in multitudo potentiae, and in robore virtutis, neque unus deest.

27 Quare dicis, Iacob, and speakis, Israel, My via est abscondita a Domino, and my judicium transivit a Deo meo?

28 Non nosti, non audivi, non intelligis; non a principio creasti te, O Israel?

29 Qui produxit in numerum exercitum coelestium, and vocavit eis omnes in nomine suo; in multitudo potentiae, and in robore virtutis, neque unus deest.

30 Non fatigatur, neque laborat; non est investigatio scientiae eius.

31 But they qui sperant in Domino, mutant fortitudinem; qui ascendant pennas, quasi aquilae; currunt, and non laborant; ambulant, and non deficient.

22 Thus dicit Dominus, Qui est Dominus vester, Qui est Sanctus Israel, Qui te redemit; I have vocatus Egyptum for te; Assyrii propter te; omnes procedent in turbam, and dicent, Ruina est, and miseriam.

23 Quia Dominus liberavit te, et redemit te; foris ergo abstersit omnes inimicos tuos; and ait, Non est ultra malum in terra, quia liberatus est tu.
