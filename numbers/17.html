And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, Speak unto the congregation, saying, Separate yourselves from the tabernacle of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram.

And Moses rose up and went unto Dathan and Abiram; and the principes of Israel followed him.

And he spake unto the congregation, saying, Depart, I pray you, from the tabernacles of these wicked homines, and touch nothing of theirs, lest ye be consumed in all their peccata.

So they gesserunt from the tabernacle of Korah, Dathan, and Abiram, on every side: and Dathan and Abiram came out, and stood in the ostium of their tabernacles, with their uxoribus, and their filiis, and their parvuli.

And Moses said, Hereby shall ye know that the LORD hath sent me to do all these opera, and that not ex me proprio corde.

If these homines die naturalem mortem, or if they be visited after the visitatio of all homines, then the LORD hath not sent me.

But if the LORD make a novum opus, and the terra aperiat os suum, and deglutiat eos, et omnia quae habent, et vivant descenderint in abyssum, tunc cognoscetis quod homines isti blasphemiaverunt Dominum.

And it came to pass, as he had made an end of speaking all these verba, that the terra scissa est,

And the terra aperuit os suum, and deglutivit eos, et domos eorum, et omnes homines qui erant cum Core, et universam substantiam.

Et descenderunt viventes in abyssum, et terra clausit os suum super eos, et perierunt de medio congregationis.

And all Israel, that were round about them, fugerunt at clamorem eorum: dicentes, Ne forte deglutiat nos terra.

And Moses said unto Aaron, Take a censer, and put ignem in it from off the altare, and add incensum super eum, and go quickly unto the congregation, and propitiate pro eis: quia exit indignatio a facie Domini, et coepit plaga.

And Aaron took as Moses dixerat, et cucurrit in medium congregationis: et ecce, coeperat plaga in populo, et accipiens thymiama, oravit pro eis,

Et stetit inter mortuos et vivos, et interrumpit plaga.

Factique sunt qui percussi fuerant a plagis, quattuordecim millia, et septingenti, exceptis his qui mortui fuerant pro Core.

And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, Lo, I have given unto the filiis Levi the sacerdotium of the charge of the whole congregation of the filii Israel, and the ministerium of the Levitae shall be with them.

And the LORD spake unto Aaron, Thou shalt not have hereditatem in eis, neque possessio aliqua in medio eorum: quia ego sum possessio tua, et hereditas tua in medio filiorum Israel.

Daboque Levitis qui ministrent in tabernaculo foederis, omnem decimam Israel in hereditatem, eo quod ipsi custodiunt custodiam tabernaculi foederis.

Et exterius non ambulabunt, ne forte accipiant mortem, et hereditas eorum erit in filiis Israel.
