And he heard the words of Laban's filius, saying, Jacob hath taken away all that was our pater's; and of that which was our pater's hath he gotten all this gloria.

And Jacob beheld the countenance of Laban, and, behold, it was not towards him as beforetime.

And the LORD said unto Jacob, Revertere unto the land of thy patres, and to thy familia; and I will be with thee.

And Jacob sent and called Rachel and Leah to the field unto his gregem;

And said unto them, I see your patris countenance, that it is not towards me as before: and the Deus of my pater hath been with me.

And ye know that with all my potentia I have served your pater.

And your pater hath deceived me, and changed my merces ten times; but Deus suffered him not to hurt me.

If he said thus, The sparsi shall be thy merces; then all the greges bare sparsi: and if he said thus, The variegati shall be thy merces; then all the greges bare variegati.

Thus Deus hath taken away the substance of your pater, and given it to me.

And it came to pass at the time that the greges concepturi sunt, that I lifted up mine oculi, and saw in somnium, and, behold, the capri which leaped upon the greges were variegati, sparsi, and albi.

And the angel of Deus spake unto me in somnium, saying, Jacob: And I said, Ecce me.

And he said, Leva oculos tuos, and see all the capri which leaped upon the greges: they are variegati, sparsi, and albi: for I have seen all that Laban doth to thee.

I am the Deus Betheli, where thou anointedst a monumentum, where thou vowedst a votum unto me: now arise, egredere out of this terra, and revertere unto the terra of thy kindred.

And Rachel and Leah answered and said unto him, Is there yet any pars or inheritance for us in our patris domus?

Are we not accounted strangers unto him? for he hath vendio us, and hath quite devorato our argentum.

For all the divitiae which Deus hath taken away from our pater, that is ours, and our filii's: now then, whatsoever Deus hath said unto thee, do.

Then Jacob arose, and lifted up his filii and his uxores upon the camelli;

And he conveyed away all his substance which he had gotten, the substance of his labor, in boves, and in pecus, and in camelos, towards Isaac his pater in the terra of Chanaan.

And Laban was gone to tondere his greges: and Rachel furata est idola of her pater.

And Jacob defecit Laban Syrum, in fugiendo, and took not a mentionem to him that he was fugiendo.

And he carried away all that he had; and he passed the flumen, and directed his faciem towards montem Gilead.

And it was told Laban in tertio die that Jacob was fugiendo.

Then Laban took his fratres with him, and pursued after him septem diebus; and they overtook him in monte Gilead.

And God came to Laban the Syrus in somnium nocte, and said unto him, Cave, ne dixeris contra Jacob quicquam bonum vel malum.

And Laban said unto Jacob, Quare clam fecisti, and furtim tulisti uxores meas et filios, quasi gladio captos?

Quare non annuntiasti mihi, ut dimiterem te cum gaudio et canticis, cum tympano et cithara?

Et non basiasti filios meos et filias meas: nunc insipiens fecisti.

Me etiam potui mittere te cum gaudio et canticis, cum tympano et cithara.

Sed nunc non mittis manum tuam ad omnes res meas, et ad omnia quae sunt mihi: fecisti ut fecisses in die furoris.

Respondit autem Jacob, et dixit: Timui, ne dicereres: Tolle filios tuos et filias tuas, quas mihi servivit, et dederis eas aliis.

Meus est omnis labor, et solicitudo mea: sed deus patris tui heri apparuit mihi, dicens: Cave ne loquaris contra Jacob quicquam durius.

Accipe ergo coram Deo, quidquid est tibi, et venerare: interrogem te, quid est quod mecum portas?

Si quaeras uxores meas, et filios, et quidquid mihi est: ecce, hic sum, vide coram Deo, et judica inter me et te.

Hic sunt septem anni, quibus servivi tibi pro Rachel filia tua: dederas mihi earn, et non accepi eam.

Servivi tibi etiam septem annis aliis pro alia filia tua: et non dedisti mihi earn.

Non feci tibi fraudem in omni ministerio tuo, et non comedi de pecore tuo, neque in sacrificio misi de boue tuo.

Fera me depredata est, et ego damnum portavi: requirere fecisti manu mea, quod furatum est diurnum et nocturnum.

Die caniculares et hiemali me calore constrictum: non fugit somnus ab oculis meis.

Hoc modo servivi tibi viginti annis in domo tua: quatuordecim annis pro filiabus tuis, et sex pro gregibus tuis: et mutasti mercedem meam decem vicibus.

Si Deus patris tui Abraham, et Deus Isaac non fuisset mecum, forsitan nunc dimisisses me vacuum: sed vidit Deus afflictionem meam, et laborem manuum mearum, et arguit in nocte ultima.

Respondit Laban, et dixit: Filiae meae, et filii mei, et greges tui, et omnia quae cernis, mea sunt, et omnia quae vides, tibi debentur: sed quid faciam filiabus meis, et filiis qui nati sunt ex eis?

Veni, et confovemus foedus, ut sit testimonium inter me et te.

Tulit ergo Jacob lapidem, et erexit cippum, dixitque fratribus suis: Afferte lapides, et faciamus tumulum, ut comedamus ibi.

Et tulerunt lapides, et fecerunt tumulum, et comederunt ibi super tumulum.

Appellavitque illum Laban tumulum testimonii, et Jacob, tumulum confirmacionis: et Laban dixit: Hic tumulus erit testimonium inter me et te, idcirco vocatus est nomine tumulus testimonii.

Videat Deus inter me et te, cum abscendero in via mea: et custodiat me Deus a te, ne quid contrarium facias mihi.

Cum ergo abiret Laban, reversusque esset ad suos: Jacob autem proficiscebat in itinere, apparuerunt ei angeli Dei, et occurrerunt ei.

Dixitque Jacob, ut videret eos: Castrum Dei est hic, et appellavit nomen loci illius, Mahanaim, id est, Castrum Dei.
