Melanchthon, Philippus, Respondit, To the most invincible, most serene, most mighty, and most puissant Emperor Charles, always August, King of the Romans, etc.

Our Confession, most serene and most mighty Emperor, which we exhibit, not in obstinacy, but with the greatest humility and sincerity, before Your Imperial Majesty.

We, Philippus Melanchthon, Pomeranus, Yngemarius, Eyben, Bacaus, and others, deputies and servants of the princes and estates, and also of the cities, which have deputed us, etc.
