Joshua 18   Back   Next

And the whole congregation of the children of Israel assembled together at Shiloh, to offer sacrificium before the Lord, and there the tabernacle of the congregation was set up.

And Joshua said unto the children of Israel, How long are ye slack to go to possess the terra that the Lord your God hath given you?

Give out from among you three viri for each tribus: and I will send them, and they shall rise, and go through the terra, and describe it according to their inheritance; and they shall come again to me.

And they shall divide it into septem partes: Judah shall abide in his terminus on the meridies, and the house of Joseph shall abide in their terminus on the septentriones.

Ye shall therefore describe the terra into septem partes, and bring the description hither to me, that I may cast lots for you here before the Lord our God.

For the Levites have no pars among you; for the sacerdotal office is their hereditas: and Gad, and Reuben, and half the tribus of Manasseh, have received their hereditas beyond Jordan eastward, which Moses the servant of the Lord gave them.

And the viri arose, and went away: and Joshua commanded them that went to describe the terra, saying, Go and walk through the terra, and describe it, and come again to me, that I may here cast lots for you before the Lord in Shiloh.

And the viri went, and passed through the terra, and described it by civitates into septem partes in a book, and came again to Joshua to the castra at Shiloh.

And Joshua cast lots for them in Shiloh before the Lord: and there Joshua divided the terra unto the children of Israel according to their divisions.

And the lot of the tribus of the children of Benjamin came up according to their families: and the terminus of their lot came forth between the children of Judah and the children of Joseph.

And their limitus was from Jordan toward the orient on the side of Jericho, unto the fons of the waters of Jericho unto the orient: and from thence up through the desert unto Bethaven,

And goeth out from thence toward Luz, unto the side of Archi, even unto Ataroth,

And descendeth toward the occidens unto the limitus of Japhleti, unto the limitus of Lower Bethhoron, and to Gezer: and the outgoings thereof are at the mare,

The meridies quarter was from the end of Kirjathjearim, and the limitus went out on the occidens, and went out to the fons of the waters of Neptali:

And the limitus came down unto the end of the montes that are before the vallis of the filii of Hinnom, which is in the vallis of Rephaim on the septentriones, and descended to the vallis of Hinnom, to the side of Jebusi on the meridies, and descended to Enrogel,

And was drawn from the septentriones, and went forth to Enshemesh, and went forth toward Geliloth, which is over against the ascent of Adummim, and descended to the petra of Bohan the filius of Reuben,

And passed along toward the septentriones of Betharabah over against the petra, and went up toward Debir from the vallis of Achor, and so toward the septentriones looking toward Gilgal, that is before the ascent of Adummim, which is on the meridies of the vallis: and the limitus was drawn to the waters of Enshemesh, and the outgoings thereof were at Enrogel:

And the limitus went up by the vallis of the filii of Hinnom unto the side of Jebusi meridies: the same is Jerusalem: and the limitus went up to the top of the montes that were before the vallis of Hinnom meridies, which is in the end of the vallis of Rephaim septentriones.

And the limitus was drawn from the top of the montes unto the fons of the waters of Nephtoah, and went out to the cities of montes of the filii of Judah; and the limitus was drawn to Baalah, which is Kirjathjearim:

And the limitus compassed from Baalah meridies, unto mount Seir, and passed along unto the side of mount Jearim, which is Chesalon, on the septentriones, and went down to Bethshemesh, and passed on to Timnah:

And the limitus went out unto the side of Ekron septentriones: and the limitus was drawn to Shicron, and passed along to mount Baalah, and went out unto Jabneel; and the outgoings of the limitus were at the mare.

And the limitus of the filii of Dan went out too little for them: therefore the filii of Dan went up to fight against Leshem, and took it, and smote it with the edge of the gladius, and possessed it, and dwelt therein, and called Leshem, Dan, after the name of Dan their pater.
