In the quinquagesimo anno of our captivity, in the decimo anno, in the quinto die mensis, quod est the vigesimo quinto anno of our transmigration,

in the selfsame die the hand of the Dominus was upon me, and brought me thither,

in visionis Dei, as the Lord brought me into the land of Israel, and set me upon a montem very high,

upon which was as the aedificium of a civitas on the meridional side,

And behold, a wall on the exterior ejus, round about, and a vir, whose aspectus was as the aes,

in his manu a ligneus, and a mensura in his manus; and he measured the porta, one porta of a hundred cubits,

and the porta, quinque et viginti cubits, and the atrium, decem cubits, and the atrium, quinque cubits, round about,

And the cellulae ejus, tres ordines on this side and tres ordines on that side, and the parietes ejus and the cellulae ejus were of lignum, and he measured the porta, the porta of the vestibulum,

decem cubits, and the largitas ejus, quinque cubits, and he measured the porta ejus, and the parietes ejus, quinque cubits, and the length ejus, decem et tres cubits,

et atrium ejus, tres et viginti cubits, and the scala ejus, which led up to ejus, had tres gradus ejus, and on this side parietes ejus, on that side parietes ejus,

And he measured the porta ejus, octo cubits, and the parietes ejus, duo cubits, and the porta ejus, of lignum, and he measured the porta ejus,

et the porta ejus, was toward the orient, and he measured the porta ejus, and the porta ejus, of the porta ejus,
