Now the sons of Issachar were, Tola, and Puah, Iobab, and Shimron, quattuor filii. The filii of Tola; Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and Iibsam, and Shemuel, head of the domus of their pater. The frater of Tola was Puah; he begat the filii of the domus of their pater. These are the familia of Issachar according to those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, fifty and septem milia and centum.

The filii of Benjamin; Bela, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard. These are the filii of Benjamin according to their familia, and those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, quadraginta milia and quingenti.

Now the filii of Dan; Hushim. The filii of Asher; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah soror eorum. The filii of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel, qui est pater of Birzavith. These are the familia of the filii of Asher according to those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, quinquaginta tria milia and quadringenti.

The filii of Naphtali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, filii Bilhah. The filii of Manasseh; Ashriel, whom she bore, but his uxor was an Aramitea. She bare also Machir pater of Gilead. And Machir took a uxor of Huppim and Shuppim, qui erant filii of Ir, and the nomen of his soror was Maachah. And the secundus filius of Machir was Zelophehad, and Zelophehad had only filias. And Maachah soror Machir eum genuit a filius, and he vocavit nomen eius Peresh, and the nomen of his frater was Sheresh, and his filii were Ulam, and Rakem. And the filius of Ulam; Bedan. These are the filii of Gilead filius of Machir filius of Manasseh. And his soror Molecheth bare Ishhod, and Abiezer, and Mahalah. And the filii of Shemida were, Ahian, and Shechem, and Likhi, and Aniam.

Et filii Ephraim according to their familia were these; Shuthelah, and Bered his filius, and Tahath his filius, and Eladah his filius, and Tahath his filius, and Zabad his filius, and Shuthelah his filius, and Ezer, and Elead, quibus occiderunt homines Gath, qui erant nati in terra, quia descenderunt ad sumere possessiones eorum. And Ephraim pater eorum ploravit multos dies, et venerunt fratres eius ut eum consolarentur. Et ingressus est ad concubinam suam, et concepit et peperit filium. Et vocavit nomen eius Beriah, quia in malis domus eius erat,

The filii of Asher according to their familia; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah soror eorum. The filii of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel, qui est pater of Birzavith. These are the familia of the filii of Asher according to those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, quinquaginta tria milia and quadringenti. Benjamin pater Belae, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, and Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard.

These are the filii of Benjamin according to their familia, and those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, quadraginta milia and quingenti. Omnes hi filii of Rachel, qui nati sunt Iacob, quadraginta sex animarum. Et filii of Bilhah ancillae Rahel; Dan and Naphtali. Et filii of Zilpah ancillae Lia; Gad and Asher. These are the filii of Iacob, qui nati sunt ei in Mesopotamia Siriae, et Dinah filia eorum, quae peperit eis filias, omnes animae filiorum eius, and filiarum, triginta tres.

And he morabat in terram, in qua peregrinabatur, in terra Chanaan, ab occidente Hamath usque ad torrentem Egypti. Ipse est qui donavit eis terminos, ab oriente usque ad mare, et ab aquilone usque ad terminos Siriae, scilicet, quinque regiones of the Philistines, and all of the Chananaei, and the Sidonii, and the Hevaei, who in the montes of Libani, from Baal Hermon usque ad ingrediendum Emath.

And the filii of Ioseph, qui nati sunt eum in terra Aegypti, Efrain, and Manasses, quos peperit eum Asenath filia Putiphare sacerdotis Heliopoleos. And the filii of Manasses; Machir, and Machir genuit Gilead, and the filii of Gilead; Peresh, and Zelophehad. And the filii of Pharez; Esrom, and Chamul. Et filii of Beniamin; Bale, and Bechor, and Asbel, and Gera, and Naaman, and Ehi, and Ros, and Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard. These are the filii of Rachel, who were geniti of Iacob, omnes animae, quadraginta sex.

And the filii of Dan; Suham, and Hushim. And the filii of Aser; Jimna, and Jesua, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah soror eorum. And the filii of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel. These are the filii of Aser, according to their familia, and those that were numbered of them, at the numerus of their viri, quinquaginta tria milia and quadringenti. The filii of Nephthali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Sellum, filii of Bilhah.

The filii of Iacob, omnes animae, qui ingrediabantur cum Iacob in Aegyptum, egressi de femore eius, exceptis uxoribus filiorum Iacob, omnes animae, sexaginta et sex. Et filii of Ioseph, qui nati sunt ei in terra Aegypti, duo animae. Omnes animae domus Iacob, quae ingrediabantur in Aegyptum, septuaginta.
