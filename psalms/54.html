Psalms 54   Back   Next

Save me, O Deus, by thy name, and judge me by thy potentia.

Hear my oratio, O Deus; give ear to the words of my os.

For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressores seek after my anima: they have not set Deus before them. Selah.

Behold, Deus is mine adiutor: the Domini that doth sustain my anima.

He shall reward evil unto mine inimici: cut them off in thy veritas.

I will freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy nomen, O Deus; for it is bonum.

For he hath delivered me out of all tribulatio; and mine oculi have seen my desiderium upon mine inimici.
