Numbers 1   Back   Next

1. And the LORD spake unto Moses in the wilderness of Sinai, in the tabernacle of the congregation, on the first day of the second mensem, in the secundus year after they were come out of the terra of Egypt, saying,

2. Take ye the sum of all the congregation of the filii Israel, after their families, by the domus of their patres, with the numerus of their nomina, every masculus from viginti years old and upward, all that are able to go forth to militia in Israel.

3. Thou and Aaron shall numerus them by their exercitus: and the principes of the domus of their patres shall be with you.

4. And with you there shall be a vir of every tribus, every one principes of the domus of his patres.

5. And these are the nomina of the viri which shall stand with you: of the tribus of Reuben; Elizur the filius of Shedeur.

6. Of Simeon; Shelumiel the filius of Zurishaddai.

7. Of Judah; Nahshon the filius of Amminadab.

8. Of Issachar; Nethaneel the filius of Zuar.

9. Of Zebulun; Eliab the filius of Helon.

10. Of the filii of Joseph: of Ephraim; Elishama the filius of Ammihud: of Manasseh; Gamaliel the filius of Pedahzur.

11. Of Benjamin; Abidan the filius of Gideoni.

12. Of Dan; Ahiezer the filius of Ammishaddai.

13. Of Asher; Pagiel the filius of Ocran.

14. Of Gad; Eliasaph the filius of Deuel.

15. Of Naphtali; Ahira the filius of Enan.

16. These were the famosi of the congregation, principes of the tribus of their patres, thousandes of Israel.

17. And Moses and Aaron took these men which are expressed by their nomina:

18. And they assembled all the congregation together on the first day of the secundus mensem, and they declared their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, by their polls.

19. As the LORD commanded Moses, so he numerus them in the wilderness of Sinai.

20. And the filii of Reuben, Israel's primogenitus, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, by their polls, every masculus from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

21. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Reuben, were xlvi thousand and quingenti.

22. Of the filii of Simeon, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, those that were numerus of them, according to the numerus of the nomina, by their polls, every masculus from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

23. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Simeon, were lix thousand and trecenti.

24. Of the filii of Gad, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

25. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Gad, were xlv thousand and sexcenti and l.

26. Of the filii of Judah, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

27. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Judah, were lxxiv thousand and sexcenti.

28. Of the filii of Issachar, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

29. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Issachar, were liv thousand and quadringenti.

30. Of the filii of Zebulun, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

31. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Zebulun, were lvii thousand and quadringenti.

32. Of the filii of Joseph, namely, of the filii of Ephraim, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

33. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Ephraim, were xl thousand and quingenti.

34. Of the filii of Manasseh, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

35. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Manasseh, were xxxii thousand and ducenti.

36. Of the filii of Benjamin, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

37. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Benjamin, were xxxv thousand and quadringenti.

38. Of the filii of Dan, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

39. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Dan, were lxii thousand and septingenti.

40. Of the filii of Asher, by their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

41. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Asher, were xli thousand and quingenti.

42. Of the filii of Naphtali, throughout their progenies, after their families, by the domus of their patres, according to the numerus of the nomina, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia;

43. Those that were numerus of them, even of the tribus of Naphtali, were liii thousand and quadringenti.

44. These are those that were numerus, which Moses and Aaron and the principes of Israel numerus, twelve men, each one for the domus of their patres.

45. And the filii of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses, so did they.

46. And all those that were numerus of the filii of Israel, according to the domus of their patres, from viginti years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to militia in Israel;

47. Even all they that were numerus were six hundred thousand and tria thousand and quingenti and l.

48. But the Levites after the tribus of their patres were not numerus among them.

49. For the LORD had spoken unto Moses, saying,

50. Only thou shalt not take the numerus of the tribus of Levi, neither shalt thou put the sum of them among the filii of Israel:

51. But thou shalt appoint the Levites over the tabernacle of testimonium, and over all the vessels thereof, and over all things that belong to it: they shall bear the tabernacle, and all the vessels thereof; and they shall minister unto it, and shall encamp round about the tabernacle.

52. And when the tabernacle setteth forward, the Levites shall take it down: and when the tabernacle is to be pitched, the Levites shall set it up: and the stranger that approacheth shall be put to death.

53. And the filii of Israel shall pitch their tents, every man by his own castra, and every man by his own signum, throughout their exercitus.

54. But the Levites shall pitch round about the tabernacle of testimonium, that there be no wrath upon the congregation of the filii of Israel: and the Levites shall keep the charge of the tabernacle of testimonium.

55. And the filii of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses, so did they.

56. And on the twentieth day of the secundus mensem, in the secundus year, the cloude was taken up from off the tabernacle of the congregation:

57. And the filii of Israel took their journeys out of the wilderness of Sinai; and the cloude rested in the wilderness of Paran.

58. And there were certus prophetes among the Levites of the filii of Israel, which did prophesy, and spake unto Moses, and unto Aaron, and unto the congregation of the Levites, saying,

59. And Moses said unto them, If it be the will of the LORD, that ye go with us, and the LORD shall give us the terra which he hath promised to give us, then we will give you the terra among us, and ye shall be unto us as the filii of Israel:

60. But if the LORD do not give us the terra, then will we not give you the terra among us, for the LORD hath spoken unto us, saying, Ye shall not go over Jordan.

61. And they said unto him, We will go into the wilderness, and pitch our tents, and we will go before you, and prepare the way before you, and we will be unto you as the filii of Israel:

62. And Moses said unto them, If ye will do this thing, if ye will go armed before the LORD to war,

63. And will go all of you armed over Jordan before the LORD, until he hath driven out his inimicos from before him,

64. And the terra be subdued before the LORD: then afterward ye shall return, and be guiltless before the LORD, and before Israel; and this terra shall be your possession before the LORD.

65. But if ye will not do so, behold, ye have sinned against the LORD: and be sure your peccatum will find you out.

66. And they said, We will go up by the montes, and we will pitch our tents before them, and we will be unto you as the filii of Israel:

67. And Moses said unto them, If ye will do this thing, if ye will go up by the montes, and prepare the way before the LORD,

68. And will go up by the montes, and drive out the inimicos of the LORD from before him,

69. And the terra be subdued before the LORD; then afterward ye shall return, and be guiltless before the LORD, and before Israel; and this terra shall be your possession before the LORD.

70. But if ye will not do so, behold, ye have sinned against the LORD: and be sure your peccatum will find you out.

71. And the filii of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses, so did they.
