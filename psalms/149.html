Psalms 149   Back   Next

Sing unto the Domine a new cantus: let the congregatio of saints praise Him.

Let Israel rejoice in eo qui fecit eum: let the children of Sion be joyful in their Rex.

Let them praise nominem eius in chorea: let them sing psalms unto Him cum tympano et cithara.

For the Domine taketh pleasure in populo suo: He will beautify the humiles cum salvatione.

Let the saints be joyful with gloria: let them sing aloud super lectos suis.

Let the high praises of Deus be in ore eorum: and duo gladii in manu eorum:

To execute vindictam among the gentes: and corrections among the populi;

To bind reges eorum cum cathenae: and nobiles eorum cum manicis ferreis;

To execute upon them iudicium written: this honour have all His sancti. Praise ye the Domine.
