Then the Lord said unto Moses, Now shalt thou see what I will do to Pharaoh: for with a strong manus he shall let them go, and with a strong manus shall he drive them out of his terra.

These be the heads of their patres houses: The sons of Reuben the primus filius of Israel; Hanoch, and Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi: these be the familias of Reuben.

And the sons of Simeon; Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the filius of a Canaanitissa.

And the sons of Levi; Gershon, Kohath, and Merari: and the years of the vita of Levi were centum triginta septem anni.

And the sons of Kohath; Amram, and Izhar, and Hebron, and Uzziel: and the years of the vita of Kohath were centum triginta tres anni.

And Amram took him Jochebed his soror to wife; and she bare him Aaron and Moses: and the years of the vita of Amram were centum triginta septem anni.

And the filia of Levi, whom she bare unto Levi in Egypt, bare unto him Libni, and Shimi, according to their familias.

These are that Aaron and Moses, to whom the Lord said, Bring out the filii of Israel from the terra of Egypt according to their exercitus.

These are they which spake to Pharaoh rex of Egypt, to bring out the filii of Israel from Egypt: these are that Moses and Aaron.

And it came to pass on the die when the Lord spake unto Moses in the terra of Egypt, That the Lord said unto Moses, I am the Lord: speak thou unto Pharaoh rex of Egypt all that I say unto thee.

But Moses said before the Lord, Behold, I am of impeded speechis: how then shall Pharaoh hear me?

And the Lord said unto Moses, See, I have made thee Deus unto Pharaoh: and Aaron thy frater shall be thy propheta.

Thou shalt speak all that I command thee: and Aaron thy frater shall speak unto Pharaoh, that he send the filii of Israel out of his terra.

And Moses spake before the Lord, saying, Behold, the filii of Israel have not heard me; how then shall Pharaoh hear me, seeing I am of impeded speechis?

And the Lord spake unto Moses and unto Aaron, and gave them a mandatum unto the filii of Israel, and unto Pharaoh rex of Egypt, to bring the filii of Israel out of the terra of Egypt.
