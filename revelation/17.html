Revelation 17   Back   Next

And there came one of the seven angels which had the seven vials, and talked with me, saying unto me, Veni, behold, I will shew thee the judicium of the great meretrix that sitteth upon many aquae.

And he carried me away in the spirit into a desert; and I saw a woman sit upon a scarlet coloured beast, full of nominibus blasphemiis, having seven capita and ten cornua.

And the woman was arrayed in purple and scarlet colour, and decked with aurum and pretiosus stones and pearls, having a golden cup in her manus, full of abomination and filthiness of her fornicatio.

And upon her forehead was a name written, Mysterium, Babylon the great, the mater of harlots and abominations of the earth.

And I saw the woman drunken with the sanguis of the sancti, and with the sanguis of the martyrs of Jesus: and when I saw her, I wondered with great admiration.

And the angel said unto me, Wherefore didst thou marvel? I will tell thee the mysterium of the woman, and of the beast that carrieth her, which hath the seven capita and ten cornua.

The beast that thou sawest was, and is not; and shall ascend out of the abyss, and go into perditio: and they that dwell on the earth shall wonder, whose names were not written in the libro vitae from the foundation of the mundus, when they behold the beast that was, and is not, and yet is.

And here is the mens sana; which hath understanding, let him count the numerus of the beast: for it is the numerus of a homo, and his numerus is Six hundred threescore and six.
