The Vulgabib

Ezekiel 24   Back   Next

1. Again in the ninth year, in the tenth month, in the tenth day of the month, the word of the Lord came unto me, saying, 2. Son of homo, scribamus hodie, in the eighth year, in the fifth month, in the fifth day of the month, as the caligo of Jerusalem was made perfect.

3. And speak unto the populus of the terra, saying, Thus saith Dominus Deus; Behold, I will put a pot upon the ignis, and I will bring it to ebullition, so as the carnes thereof be cooked, and the broth thereof be drained off.

4. Gather the ossa thereof, and set it upon the ignis, and seethe the broth thereof, and let the ossa thereof be cooked in the medio thereof.

5. Take a great caldron, and set it on, and also pour aqua into it, and gather the fragmenta of the carnes thereof into it, and every good pars thereof, the coxa and the shoulder, take them and put them in the caldron.

6. Then take the caldron, and put it upon the ignis, and seethe it, and let the carnes thereof be cooked, and the broth thereof be drained off, and let the ossa thereof be gathered, and burned with ignis.

7. And thou, son of homo, thus saith Dominus Deus; To the montes of Israel, and to the colles, and to the vallis, and to every rivus, say, Thus saith Dominus Deus; Behold, I will bring upon you the gladius, and I will destroy your excelsa, and your loci alti.
