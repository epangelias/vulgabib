Psalms 97   Back   Next

Jehovah reigneth, let the earth rejoice; let the multitude of isles be glad thereof.

Clouds and darkness are round about him: justitia and judicium are the habitation of his throne.

A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about.

His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled.

The hills melted like wax at the presence of Jehovah, at the presence of the Dominus of the whole earth.

The heavens have declared his justitia, and all the people have seen his gloria.

Confundantur omnes qui serviant sculptili, qui gloriantur in idolis: adorabunt eum omnes dii Sion.

Zion heard, and was glad; and the daughters of Judah rejoiced, because of thy judicia, O Jehovah.

Quoniam tu, Jehovah, Altissimus es super omnem terram, vehementer exaltatus es super omnes dii.

Qui oditis malum, vos qui diligitis Jehovah, custodite animam meam; a manu peccatoris liberabit me Jehovah.

Luminaria pura sunt labia justi, sed os impiorum proditione operabitur.

Gaudete, justi, in Jehovah, et celebrabitis memoriam sanctitatis ejus.

Cantate Domino canticum novum, quia mirabilia fecit: salus facta est dextera ejus, et sanctitas ejus.

Jehovah salvavit dexteram suam, et audentia ejus audivit in terra nostra.

Salus justorum a Domino, et protector eorum in tempore tribulationis.

Et adjutor eorum in die afflictionis, quia in Jehovah speraverunt, et salvabit eos, quia ipse eos salvabit, quia in eo speraverunt.
