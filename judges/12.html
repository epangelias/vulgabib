And Ephraim said unto him, Why hast thou served us thus, seeing that I have magnanimus spiritus, and I exhortavi the people, and thou hast not vocatus me to be with the exercitus?

Then the mens of Ephraim said unto him, What hast thou therefore now? Say now in this: If thou hast magnanimus spiritus, why hast thou humiliatus nos, and not vocatus me to be with the exercitus?

And he said unto them, What have I to do with thee, O thou garrula Ephraim? thou art as a avis which fugit before the leo: for when I clamavi in Monte Ephraim, who would ascend with me to the exercitus? and the Domini non respondit.

Then said they unto him, Say now Shibboleth: and he said Sibboleth: for he could not recte pronuntiare it. Then they took him, and mactaverunt him at the vada Jordan: and there ceciderunt at that time of the Ephraimites quadraginta duo milia.

And Jephthah judicavit Israel sex annos: then died Jephthah, and sepultus est in civitate sua, Gilead.

And he had triginta filii, qui equitabant triginta asinos, et habuerunt triginta civitates, quae vocantur Havoth Jair usque ad hodie in terra Gilead.

And Abdon filius Hillel Ahohite judicavit Israel, et habuit quadraginta filios, et triginta nepotes, qui equitabant septuaginta asinos pueros.

And Abdon filius Hillel Ahohite judicavit Israel octo annos, and he died, and sepultus est in Pirathon in terra Ephraim, in Monte Amalechite.

And after him judicavit Israel Abimelech filius Jerobaal, and he judicavit Israel tres annos, and he died, and sepultus est in Arumah, et regnavit post eum Thola filius Puah, qui fuit ex semine Issachar, and he dwelt in Samir in Monte Ephraim.

And he judicavit Israel viginti tres annos, and he died, and sepultus est in Samir: and after him judicavit Israel Jair filius Manasse, qui judicavit Israel viginti duo annos.
