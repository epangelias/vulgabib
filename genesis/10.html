Genesis 10   Back   Next

And the sons of Noe, going out of the arca, were Sem, Ham, and Japheth. And Ham is the pater of Chanaan.

These are the filii of Noe: and of them was the orbis terrarum repeopled after the diluvium.

And the filii of Japheth: Gomer, and Magog, and Madai, and Javan, and Tubal, and Meshech, and Tiras.

And the filii of Gomer: Aschkenaz, and Riphath, and Togarmah.

And the filii of Javan: Elishah, and Tarshish, Kittim, and Dodanim.

The filii of Ham: Chus, and Misraim, and Put, and Chanaan.

And the filii of Chus: Saba, and Havilah, and Sabtah, and Raamah, and Sabtecha. And the filii of Raamah: Seba, and Dedan.

And Chus begat Nimrod: qui coepit esse potens in terra.

He was a venator before the Domino: wherefore it is said: sicut Nimrod venator before the Domino.

And the beginning of his regnum was Babylon, and Arach, and Achad, and Chalanne in terra Sennaar.

Out of that land came Assur, and built Ninive, and the streets of the city, and Chalach:

And Dase, between Ninive and Chalach: this is the great civitas.

And Misraim begat Ludim, and Anamim, and Lehabim, and Naphtuhim:

And Pathrusim, and Casluhim, out of whom came the Philistim, and Caphtorim.

And Chanaan begat Sidon his primogenitum, and the Hethite:

And the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgasite, and the Hevite, and the Arakite, and the Sinite:

And the Aradian, and the Samarite, and the Hamathite: and afterward the families of the Chanaanite were spread abroad.

And the border of the Chanaanite was from Sidon, as thou goest to Gerar, unto Gaza: as thou goest to Sodoma, and Gomorra, and Adama, and Seboim, unto Lesa.

These are the filii of Ham, according to their families, and their linguae, in their terrae, and in their gentibus.
