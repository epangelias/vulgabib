Psalms 47   Back   Next

O clap your manus, all ye gentes: shout unto Deus with voces of laetitia.

For Dominus is Altissimus, and terribilis: a great Rex over all terram.

He shall subjugare gentes under us, and populos under nostra pedes.

He shall chuse nostra hereditas: the beauty of Iacob whom He loved.

Deus is ascended with iubilus, Dominus with voces of Buccina.

Sing ye praises to Deus, sing praises: sing praises unto nostro Rex, sing praises.

For Deus is Rex of all terram: sing ye praises with intellectus.

Deus sitteth upon His sanctus thronus, Dominus shall iudge gentes.

Principes of gentes are congregated together unto populus of Deus of Abraham: for Dei sunt scuta terrae: He is magnificently elevated.
