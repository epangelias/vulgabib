Yet now hear, O Jacob my servant; and Israel, whom I have chosen: Thus saith Dominus that made thee, and formed thee from uterus, that will help thee; Fear not, O Jacob, my servant; and thou Jeshurun, whom I have electus.

For I will pour aqua upon him that is sitis, and floods upon the terra that is arida: I will pour my Spirit upon thy semen, and my blessing upon thine offspring:

And they shall spring up as among the herbæ, as willows by the fluvius of aqua.

One shall say, I am Dominus'; another shall call himself by the nomen of Jacob; and another shall subscribe with his manus unto Dominus, and surname himself by the nomen of Israel.

Thus saith Dominus the King of Israel, and his Redemptor the Dominus of hosts; I am the First, and I am the Last; and beside me there is no Deus.

Fear ye not, neither be afraid: have not I told thee from that tempus, and have declared it? ye are even my testes. Is there a Deus beside me? yea, there is no Rock; I know not any.

They that make a simulacrum are all of them vanitas; and their delectation shall not profit; and they are their own testes; they see not, nor know; that they may be ashamed.

Who hath formed a Deus, or molten a simulacrum that is profitable for nothing?

Behold, all his companions shall be ashamed: and the fabri themselves are but homines: let them all be gathered together, let them stand up; yet they shall fear, and they shall be ashamed together.

The faber of ferrum sharpeneth a ferrum instrument, and worketh in the ignis; the faber of ferrum maketh an instrument for his work; and he worketh in the ferrum, and in the lignum, and in the flamma; and he maketh it in the forma of a simulacrum.

The faber of ferrum hath wrought it with the ferrum, and hath worked in the flamma: the faber of ferrum hath made all his work with the ferrum; and he hath wrought it with the ferrum, and in the lignum.

The faber of ferrum is athirst, and his animus fainted: he hath drunk no aqua, and he is faint.

The faber of lignum stretcheth out his manus, and taketh the ferrum, and worketh in the lignum, and maketh it in the forma of a simulacrum.

He hath wrought it in the forma of a vir, and in the forma of a femina, and in the forma of a puella, and in the forma of a puer.

He hath made also simulacra of lignum, and simulacra of ferrum, and simulacra of lapidum, and simulacra of argilla.

He hath made simulacra of all the genera of bestiæ, and simulacra of all the genera of volucres, and simulacra of all the genera of pisces.

He hath made simulacra of all the genera of reptiliæ, and simulacra of all the genera of insecta.

He hath made simulacra of all the genera of arborum, and simulacra of all the genera of herbæ.

He hath made simulacra of all the genera of florum, and simulacra of all the genera of fructuum.

Thus saith Dominus, Thy Redemptor, and thy Creator from uterus, I am Dominus, that maketh omnia; that stretcheth forth the cælum alone; that spreadeth abroad the terra by myself;

That frustrateth the signa of the pseudoprophetæ, and maketh the divini foolish, that turneth wise men backward, and maketh their scientia foolishness;

That confirmeth the sermonem of his servus, and performeth the consilium of his messis: that saith to Jerusalem, Thou shalt be inhabited; and to the civitas of Judah, Ye shall be built, and I will raise up the ruinas thereof;

That saith to the abyssus, Be dry, and I will dry up thy flumina: That saith of Cyrus, He is my pastor, and shall perform all my voluntas, even saying to Jerusalem, Thou shalt be built; and to the templum, Thy fundamenta shall be laid.

Thus saith Dominus to his Messiah, to Cyrus, whose manus I have holden, to subdue gentes before him; and I will loose the cingulum of kings, to open before him the portæ that shall not be shut;

I will go before thee, and make the crooked ways straight: I will break in pieces the portæ of æræ, and cut in sunder the vectes of ferrum:

I will give thee the thesauros of tenebris, and hidden riches of secret places, that thou mayest know that I, Dominus, which call thee by thy nomen, am the Deus of Israel.

For Jacob my servant's sake, and Israel mine electus, I have even called thee by thy nomen: I have surnamed thee, though thou hast not known me.

I am Dominus, and there is none else, there is no Deus beside me: I girded thee, though thou hast not known me:

That they may know from the rising of the sol to the going down of the sol, that there is none beside me. I am Dominus, and there is none else.

I form the lumen, and create the tenebræ: I make pacem, and create malum: I Dominus do all these things.

Drop down, ye cæli, from above, and let the nubes rain justitiam: let the terra open, and let salvation germinate, and let justitia spring up together; I Dominus have created it.

Woe unto him that striveth with his Creator! Let the potsherd strive with the potsherds of the terra. Shall the clay say to him that fashioneth it, What makest thou? or thy work, He hath no manus?

Woe unto him that saith unto his pater, What begettest thou? or to the mater, What hast thou brought forth?

Thus saith Dominus, the Holy One of Israel, and his Creator, Ask me of things to come concerning my filii, and concerning the work of my manus, command ye me.

I have made the terra, and created man upon it: I, even my manus, have stretched out the cæli, and all their host have I commanded.

I have raised him up in justitia, and I will direct all his via: he shall build my civitas, and he shall let go my captivus, not for price nor reward, saith Dominus of hosts.

Thus saith Dominus, The labour of Egypt, and merchandise of Ethiopia and of the Sabeans, men of stature, shall come over unto thee, and they shall be thine: they shall come after thee; in chains they shall come over, and they shall fall down unto thee, they shall make supplication unto thee, saying, Surely Deus is in thee; and there is none else, there is no Deus.

Verily thou art a Deus that hidest thyself, O Deus of Israel, the Salvator.

They shall be ashamed, and also confounded, all of them: they shall go to confusion together that are makers of idols.

But Israel shall be saved in Dominus with an æterna salvation: ye shall not be ashamed nor confounded world without end.

For thus saith Dominus that created the cæli; Deus himself that formed the terra and made it; he hath established it, he created it not in vanum, he formed it to be inhabited: I am Dominus, and there is none else.

I have not spoken in secret, in a dark place of the terra: I said not unto the semen of Jacob, Seek ye me in vanum: I Dominus speak justitiam, I declare things that are right.

Assemble yourselves and come; draw near together, ye that are escaped of the gentes: they have no scientia that set up the lignum of their simulacrum, and pray unto a Deus that cannot save.

Tell ye, and bring them near; yea, let them take counsel together: who hath declared this from antiquus? who hath told it from that tempus? have not I Dominus? and there is no Deus else beside me; a justus Deus and a Salvator; there is none beside me.

Look unto me, and be saved, all the fines of the terra: for I am Deus, and there is none else.

I have sworn by myself, the verbum is gone out of my os in justitia, and shall not return, That unto me every genu shall bow, every lingua shall swear.

Surely, shall one say, in Dominus have I justitia and strength: even to him shall men come; and all that are incensed against him shall be ashamed.

In Dominus shall all the semen of Israel be justified, and shall glory.
