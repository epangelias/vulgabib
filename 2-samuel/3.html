And it came to pass, after the mortuus of Saul, when David was returned from the victoria over the Amalekites, that David remained two dies in Ziklag.

And it came to pass on the tertius dies, that behold, a vir came out of the castrum from Saul, with his vestes rent, and terra upon his caput: and when he came to David, he cecidit to the terra, and adoravit.

And David said unto him, From whence comest thou? And he said unto him, I am escaped out of the castrum of Israel.

And David said unto him, How went the res, I pray thee? Tell me. And he answered, That the populus is fled from the proelium, and many of them are mortui; and Saul and Jonathas filius eius sunt interfecti, and David was afflictus.

And David said unto the puer, Qui te docuit, saying, Saul and Jonathas filius eius sunt mortui? And the puer responded, I happened by chance upon montem Gilboa, and, behold, Saul leaned upon his hasta, and, lo, the currus and equites followed hard after him.

And when he looked behind him, he saw me, and called unto me. And I answered, Ecce. And he said unto me, Qui es tu? And I answered him, I am an Amalekite.

He said unto me again, Stas, and interfice me, for angustia is come upon me, because my vita is yet whole in me. So I stood upon him, and interfeci him, because I wist that he could not vivere after that he was fallen: and I took the diadema that was upon his caput, and the armilla that was on his manus, and have brought them hither unto my dominus.

And David took hold on his vestes, and cecidit upon his facies, and adoravit, and he said, Benedicite Domino, who hath judicium of the manum meam, and hath tradituum meum inimicum in manus meas.

And David called one of the juvenis, and said, Accede, and interfice eum. And he struck him, that he mortuus est. And David dixit unto him, Thy sanguis be upon thy caput, for thy os hath testimonium against thee, saying, I have interfecit the Christus of the Domini.
