1 John 3   Back   Next

Behold, what manner of amor our Pater hath bestowed upon us, that we should be called the filii of Deus: therefore the world knoweth us not, because it knew Him not.

Beloved, now are we the filii of Deus, and it doth not yet appear what we shall be: but we know that, when He shall appear, we shall be like Him; for we shall see Him as He is.

Et quicumque habet spem hanc in eo, purificat se, sicut et ille purus est.

And every man that hath this spes in Him, purifieth himself, even as He is purus.

Whosoever committeth peccatum transgresseth also the lex: for peccatum is the transgression of the lex.

And ye know that He was manifested to take away peccata: and in Him is no peccatum.

Whosoever abideth in Him, sinneth not: whosoever sinneth, hath not seen Him, neither known Him.

Little children, let no man deceive you: he that doth iustitia is iust, even as He is iust.

He that committeth peccatum is of the Diabolus; for the Diabolus sinneth from the principio. For this purpose the Filius Dei was manifested, that He might destroy the works of the Diabolus.

Whosoever is born of Deus, doth not commit peccatum; for his seed remaineth in him: and he cannot sin, because he is born of Deus.

In hoc cognoscimus, quia cognovimus eum, et hic est amor, quia cognovimus eum et mandata eius servimus.

In this the charity of Deus was manifested toward us, because Deus sent His only begotten Filius into the world, that we might live through Him.

In hoc est amor, non quod nos dileximus Deus, sed quod ipse dilexit nos, et misit Filius suum propitiationem pro peccatis nostris.

Beloved, if Deus so loved us, we ought also to love one another.

No man hath seen Deus at any time. If we love one another, Deus abideth in us, and His amor is perfected in us.

Herein is our amor made perfect, that we may have fiducia in the die iudicii: because as He is, so are we in this mundus.

There is no timor in amor; but perfectum amor casteth out timor: because timor hath torment. He that feareth is not made perfect in amor.

We love Him, because He first loved us.

If some man say, I love Deus, and hateth his brother, he is a liar: for he that loveth not his brother whom he hath seen, how can he love Deus whom he hath not seen?

And this commandment have we from Him, That he who loveth Deus love his brother also.
