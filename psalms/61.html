Psalms 61   Back   Next

Hear my cry, O Deus, attend unto my prayer.

From the ends of the terra, I will cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the petra that is higher than I.

For thou hast been a refuge for me, a tour of strength from the face of the enemy.

I will dwell in thy tabernacula for ever: I will be holden in the covert of thy wings.

For thou, O Deus, hast heard my votum: thou hast given hereditatem to those that fear thy Nomen.
