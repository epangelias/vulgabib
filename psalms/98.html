O sing unto the Domini a nova cantus, for He hath done magnam salvationem, His dextera and sanctum brachium hath gotten Him victoriam.

Domini has made known His salus, before the eyes of gentes He hath revealed His justitia.

He hath remembered His miscericordia and veritas towards the domus of Israel, omnes fines terrae have seen salvatorem of our Deus.

Iubilate Domino, omnis terra, emittite vocem gaudii et cantus, Iubilate Domino, resonantes, cymbalis Iubilate.

Iubilate Domino in psalterio, et lyra, in lyra, et cithara, quia omnis honor terrae suum cum exsultatione serviet Domino.

Bucina cum voci Iubilate Domino.

Mare, et implevere, mundus et omnes habitantes, rivi erunt scutem contra cum ipse pro Iudice dictus cum irato intuetur adversariis, calorem ipse nescient.
