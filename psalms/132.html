Psalms 132   Back   Next

Lord, remember David, and all his afflictiones,

How he sware unto the Lord, and vowed unto the Deus of Jacob,

Surely I will not come into the tabernaculum of my domus, nor go up into my lectus;

I will not give somnus to mine oculi, or slumber to mine eyelids;

Until I find out a locum for the Lord, an habitation for the Deus of Jacob.

Lo, we heard of it at Ephratah: we found it in the nemus of the forest;

We will go into his tabernaculum: we will worship at his pedes.

Arise, O Lord, into thy requies: thou, and the arca of thy potentia.

Let thy sacerdotes be clothed with iustitia; and let thy sancti rejoice;

For thy servus David's sake turne not away the facies of thine unctus.

The Lord hath sworn in veritate unto David; he will not turne from it; Of the fructus of thy ventris will I set upon thy thronus.

If thy filii will keep my pactum and my testimonium that I shall teach them, their filii shall also sit upon thy thronus for evermore.

For the Lord hath elected Sion; he hath desired it for his habitaculum.

This shall be my requies for ever: here will I dwell; for I have desired it.

I will abundantly bless her victum: I will satisfie her pauperes with panis.

I will also clothe her sacerdotes with salvus: and her sancti shall shout for iucunditas.

There will I make the potentia of David to ascend: I have ordained a lucerna for mine unctus.

His inimici I will clothe with confusio: but upon himself shall his coronet florish.
