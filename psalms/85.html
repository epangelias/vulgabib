Psalms 85   Back   Next

Bow down thine auris, O Deus, and hear: open thine oculus, and see; and hear my vox.

For I will dire for thee, and thy populus Israel, that they may give thee donum and offer sacrificium.

Thou hast shown favour unto thy terra, O Deus; thou hast brought back captivus of Jacob.

Thou hast forgiven iniquitas of thy populus, thou hast covered omnis peccatum.

Selah.

Take away all ira, and quiesce indignatio: and be thou pacificus.

Turn us, O Deus salutaris, and cause thy facies to shine.

Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy populus may rejoice in thee?

Shew us thy misericordia, O Deus, and grant us thy salus.

I will hear what Deus the Dominus will speak: for he will speak pacem unto his populus, and to his sancti: but let them not turn again to stultitia.

Surely his salus is nigh them that timorem Deum; that gloria may habitare in terra nostra.

Misericordia and veritas are met together; justitia and pax have kissed each other.

Veritas shall flourish out of terra; and justitia shall look down from caelum.

Yea, Deus shall give what is bonum; and terra nostra shall give her fructus.

Justitia shall go before him; and shall set in via gressus ejus.
