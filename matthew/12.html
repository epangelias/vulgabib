At that time Jesus went on the sabbatum, and his disciples were famem habentes, they began to pluck the spicae of the hordeum, and to vesci. And the pharisaei, seeing it, said unto him, Behold, thy disciples do that which it is not licitum to do upon the sabbatum.

But he said unto them, Have ye not lectus what David did when he and they that were with him were esurientes, how he entered into the domus Dei, and did eat the panis propositionis, which it was not licitum for him to eat, neither for them which were with him, but only for the sacerdotes?

Or have ye not lectus in the lex, how that on the sabbatum the sacerdotes in the templum profanant the sabbatum, and are sine reatu? But I say unto you, That in this loco is one greater than the templum.

But if ye had known what this significat, I will have misericordiam, and not sacrificium, ye would not have condemnatos the innocentes. For the Filius hominis is Dominus also of the sabbatum.

And when he was departed thence, he went into their synagoga, and behold, there was a homo having one manus contractam. And they asked him, saying, Is it licitum to sanare on the sabbatum? that they might accusare him.

And he said unto them, What homo shall there be inter vos, that shall have one ovem, and if it fall into a fovea on the sabbatum, will he not manu apprehendere it, and levare it? How much then is a homo melior than an ovem? Wherefore it is licitum to benefacere on the sabbatum.

Then saith he to the homo, Extende manum tuam. And he extended it; and it was restaurata, ut alia.

Then the pharisaei went out, and held a concilium against him, how they might perdere eum. But when Jesus knew it, he secessit thence, and multae turbae secutae sunt eum, et sanavit omnes:

And charged them that they should not manifestare him: That it might be impletum that which was dictum by Esaias propheta, saying, Ecce, puer meus, quem elegi, dilectus meus, in quo bene complacuit mihi, ponam spiritum meum super eum, et iudicium gentibus adnuntiabit.

He shall not contend, neque clamabit, neque audietur quis clamor eius in plateis. Calamos quassos non confringet, et linum fumigans non extinguet, donec eiciat in victoriam iudicium.

In nomine eius gentes sperabunt. Et venit Jesus, et discipuli eius in castellum Caesareae Philippi. Et coepit interrogare discipulos suos, dicens, Quem dicunt homines esse me Filium hominis?

Qui dixerunt, Alii, Ioannem Baptistam, alii autem, Heliam, alii vero, Ieremiam, aut unum ex prophetis.

Dicit illis, Vos autem, quem me esse dicitis? Respondens Simon Petrus, dixit, Tu es Christus, Filius Dei vivi.

Respondens autem Jesus, dixit, Beatus es, Simon Bar Iona, quia caro et sanguis non revelaverunt tibi, sed Pater meus, qui in coelis est. Et ego dico tibi, quia tu es Petrus, et super hanc petram aedificabo ecclesiam meam, et portae inferi non praevalebunt adversus eam.

Dabo tibi claves regni coelorum. Et quodcumque ligaveris super terram, erit ligatum et in coelis: et quodcumque solveris super terram, erit solutum et in coelis.

Tunc praecepit discipulis suis, ut nemini dicerent quia ipse esset Iesus Christus. Ab illa die coepit Iesus ostendere discipulis suis, quia oportebat eum ire Hierosolymam, et multa pati a senioribus, et a pontificibus, et a scribis, et occidi, et tertia die resurgere.

Et assumens eum Jesus, dixit, Vae tibi, Satana, quia non sapis, quae Dei sunt, sed, quae hominum. Tunc ait Iesus Petro, Vade post me, Satana, scandalum es mihi, quia non sapis, quae Dei sunt, sed, quae hominum.

Qui conversus, dixit Petro, Recede a me, Satana, quia non sapis, quae Dei sunt, sed, quae hominum.

Ab illa die coepit Iesus ostendere discipulis suis, quia oportebat eum ire Hierosolymam, et multa pati a senioribus, et a pontificibus, et a scribis, et occidi, et tertia die resurgere.

In illo tempore accesserunt ad Iesum discipuli, dicentes, Quis, putas, maior est in regno coelorum? Et advocatis, pueris duobus, imposuit manus super eos, dicens, Amen, dico vobis, nisi convertamini, et efficiamini sicut parvuli, non intrabitis in regnum coelorum.

Qui ergo se humiliaverit sicut parvulus iste, hic maior est in regno coelorum. Et qui susceperit unum parvulum talem in nomine meo, me suscipit.
