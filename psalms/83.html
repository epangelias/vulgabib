1 O God, do not keep silence: hold not thy peace, and be not still, Deus meus, ne taceas.

2 For, behold, thine enemies make a tumult: and they that hate thee have magnificus, lifted up their head.

3 They have taken crafty counsel against thy people, and consulted against thy hidden ones.

4 They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a gens: that the name of Israel may be no more in memoria.

5 For they have consulted together with one consent: they are in foedus against thee:

6 The tabernacles of Edom, and the Ishmaelites; of Moab, and the Hagarenes;

7 Gebal, and Ammon, and Amalek; the Philistines with the inhabitants of Tyre;

8 Assur also is joined with them: they have holpen the children of Lot. Selah.

9 Do unto them as unto the Midianites; as to Sisera, as to Jabin, at the brook of Kison:

10 Which perished at En-dor: they became as dung for the terra.

11 Make their nobles like Oreb, and like Zeeb: yea, all their princes as Zebah, and as Zalmunna:

12 Who said, Let us take to ourselves the houses of Deus in hereditas.

13 O my Deus, make them like aè½®: like the stubble before the facies.

14 As the flamma burneth a forest, and as the ignis setteth the mountains on smoke;

15 So persecute them with thy tempest, and make them afraid with thy procella.

16 Fill their facies with confusion: that they may seek thy nomine, O Deus.

17 Let them be confounded and troubled for ever; yea, let them be put to shame, and perish:

18 That men may know that thou, whose nomine alone is Jehova, art the Altissimus over all the terra.
