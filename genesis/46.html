And Israel took his journey with all that he had, and came to Beersheba, and offered sacrificia to Deus pater of his father Isaac.

And Deus appeared unto Israel in a visio nocturna, and said, Jacob, Jacob. And he said, Here am I.

And he said, I am Deus, Deus of thy father: fear not to descend into Aegyptum; for I will there make thee into a great gens.

And Jacob rose up from Beersheba: and the filii of Israel carried Jacob their pater, and their parvuli, and their mulieres, in the carrucis which Pharaoh had sent to carry him.

And they took their greges, and their substantia, which they had acquired in terra Chanaan, and came into Aegyptum, Jacob, and all his semen with him:

His filii, and the filii of his filii with him, his filiae, and the filiae of his filii with him, and all his semen brought he with him into Aegyptum.

And these are the nomina of the filii of Israel, who came into Aegyptum, Jacob and his domus:

Reuben, the primogenitus of Jacob; the filii of Reuben; Hanoch, and Phallu, and Hezron, and Carmi.

The filii of Simeon; Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the filius of a Canaanitissa.

And the filii of Levi; Gershon, Kohath, and Merari.

And the filii of Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah, and Pharez, and Zarah: but Er and Onan died in terra Chanaan. And the filii of Pharez were Hezron, and Hamul.

The filii of Issachar; Tola, and Phuvah, and Job, and Shimron.

And the filii of Zebulun; Sered, and Elon, and Jahleel.

These be the filii of Leah, whom she bare unto Jacob in Paddan-aram, with Dinah the filia of Leah, which she bare unto Jacob: all the animae of his filii and filiae were thirty and three.

And the filii of Gad; Ziphion, and Haggi, Shuni, and Ezbon, Eri, and Arodi, and Areli.

And the filii of Asher; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and Beriah, and Serah their soror: and the filii of Beriah; Heber, and Malchiel.

These are the filii of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to Leah his filia, and these she bare unto Jacob; even sixteen animae.

The filii of Rachel Jacob's uxor; Joseph, and Benjamin.

And unto Joseph in terra Aegypti were born Manasseh and Ephraim, whom Asenath the filia of Potipherah sacerdos of On bare unto him.

And the filii of Benjamin were Belah, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard.

These are the filii of Rachel, which were born to Jacob: all the animae were fourteen.

And the filii of Bilhah Rachel's ancilla; Dan, and Naphtali.

And the filii of Dan; Hushim.

And the filii of Naphtali; Jahzeel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shillem.

These are the filii of Bilhah, whom Laban gave unto Rachel his filia, and she bare these unto Jacob: all the animae were seven.

All the animae that came with Jacob into Aegyptum, which came out of his lumbus, besides Jacob's uxor filii, were sixty and six;

And the filii of Joseph, which were born him in terra Aegypti, were two animae: all the animae of the domus of Jacob, which came into Aegyptum, were seventy.

And he sent Judah before him unto Joseph, to direct his facies unto Gosen, and they came into terra Gosen.

And Joseph made ready his currus, and went up to meet Israel his pater, unto Gosen, and presented himself unto him; and he fell on his collum, and wept on his collum a good while.

And Israel said unto Joseph, Now let me mori, since I have seen thy facies, because thou art yet vivus.

And Joseph said unto his fratres, and unto the domus of his pater, I will ascend, and adnuntiate Pharaoni, and say unto him, My fratres, and the domus of my pater, which were in terra Chanaan, are come unto me;

And the homines are pastores, and their negotium is to feed animas; and they have brought their ovium, and their boves, and all that they have.

And it shall be, when Pharaoh shall call you, and shall say, What is your negotium?

That ye shall say, Thy servos have been pastores from their iuventus even until now, both we, and also our patres: that ye may dwell in terra Gosen; for every pastor is abominatio to Aegyptios.
