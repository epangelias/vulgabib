Psalms 51   Back   Next

1 Have mercy upon me, O Deus, according to thy misericordia, according unto the multitude of thy benignitas blot out my peccata.

2 Wash me throughly from mine iniquitas and cleanse me from my peccatum.

3 For I acknowledge my peccata and my peccatum is ever before me.

4 Against thee, thee only, have I peccatum and done this malum in thy sight that thou mightest be iustus in thy words and pure in thy iudicium.

5 Behold, I was shapen in iniquitate and in peccato did my mater conceive me.

6 Behold, thou desirest veritatem in the interiora and in the absconditis sapientia shalt thou make me to know.

7 Purge me with hyssopus and I shall be mundus wash me and I shall be albus than nix.

8 Make me to hear iucunditatem and laetitiam that the ossa which thou hast contritum may rejoice.

9 Hide thy facies from my peccata and blot out all mine iniquitates.

10 Create in me a cor mundum, O Deus and renew a spiritus rectus in me.

11 Cast me not away from thy facies and take not thy Spiritus Sanctus from me.

12 Restore unto me the iucunditatem of thy salvus and hold me up with thy Spiritus volantis.

13 Then will I teach transgressores thy vias and peccatores shall be converted unto thee.

14 Deliver me from sanguis, O Deus, Deus meus and my lingua shall sing alacritas thy iustitia.

15 O Deus, Deus meus open my labia and my os shall shew forth thy laudem.

16 For thou desirest not sacrificium, inquit, aut ego dabo and whole burnt offrenda for inmolandum non requiras.

17 The sacrificia Deus of Deum a contritum spiritus contritum et humiliatum cor non sprevisti, Deus.

18 Do good in thy benignitate to Sion, build thou the muri Ierusalem.

19 Then shalt thou accept sacrificium, and offrendam et holocaustum et totum right ductorem offeretur et Iustorum Sacrificia then upon thine Altare imponimus Vitulos.
