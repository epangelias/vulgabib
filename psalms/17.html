Psalms 17   Back   Next

Hear the right, O LORD, attend unto my cry, give ear unto my prayer, that goeth not out of feigned lips.

Let my sentence come forth from thy presence; let thine eyes behold the things that are equal, veritas iniquitatis.

Thou hast proved mine cor, thou hast visited me in nocte; thou hast tried me, and shalt find nothing; I am purposed that my os shall not transgress.

Concerning the works of homines, by the word of thy labia, I have kept me from the via violentorum.

Hold up my gressus in thy viis, that my pedes slip not.

I have called upon thee, for thou shalt hear me, O Deus; inclina aurem tuam ad me, and exaudi verba mea.

Shew thy mirabilis misericordiam, qui salvos facis sperantes in te, a facie adversariorum.

Keep me as pupillam oculi; absconde me sub umbra alarum tuarum, a facie impiorum, qui me affligunt, inimici mei animam meam.

They are incircumcisi in cordibus suis; they shall be fatui, et non poterunt invenire viam.

Absorbebat eos, quia in abundantia gaudii sui, et in nocte dormiebant in vinculis, et erant in laqueis a te positis.

Quia tu, Deus, meus auxilium, et refugium meum es tu.

O Deus, misericordia tua praecedat me; et gloria tua protegat me.

Let me not be taken in laqueis, quos statuerunt mihi; et laquei, quos posuerunt mihi, capiant eos.

Quia tu, Deus, es spes mea; Domine, in te confido ab infantia mea.

A ventre matris meae tu es protector meus; in te speravi a ventre; ab ubere matris meae tu es qui me portas.

Non avertas faciem tuam a me; in die, quando angustia mea est, inclina aurem tuam ad me; in die, quando invoco te, velociter exaudi me.
