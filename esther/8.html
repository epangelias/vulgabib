On that day did the king Ahasuerus give the house of Haman the Jews' enemy unto Esther the queen. And Mordecai came before the king, for Esther had told what he was unto her.

Et dedit rex Ahasuerus Esther regina domum Haman inimicum Judaeorum. And Mordecai came before the king, for Esther had told what he was unto her.

The king took off his ring, cuius signaculum, which he had taken from Haman, and gave it unto Mordecai. And Esther set Mordecai over the house of Haman.

Et dixit Esther: Si placet regi, et si videor invenire gratiam in conspectu regis, et si regi placuerit, detur me scriptura, ut contradicant literae, quas scripsit Haman filius Hamadatha Agagites, qui scripsit, ut interficeret Judaeos, qui erant in regno regis.

Et scripsit rex Ahasuerus scribas, qui serviebant in omnibus provinciis, a India usque Aegyptum, et ceteris centum viginti septem provinciis, ut scriberent, quae volebat, unicuique provinciae secundum scripturam, et unicuique populo secundum linguam suam, et Judaeis secundum scripturam suam, et secundum linguam suam.

Et dedit rex Ahasuerus Esther regina domum Haman inimicum Judaeorum. And Mordecai came before the king, for Esther had told what he was unto her.

Et posuit rex anulum suum, cuius signaculum, in manu Mordechi. And Esther set Mordecai over the house of Haman.

Et scripsit Esther regina filia Mardochei, et Mardocheus Judaeus, qui erat secundus post regem Ahasuerum, scripsit omnia, quae volebat, de Judaeis in regno Ahasueri, et dedit literas, quas scripsit, ut salvi essent Judaei in regno Ahasueri, et dans eis potestatem, ut ultionem sumerent de hostibus suis.

Et posuit rex Ahasuerus Judaeis, qui erant in Susis, literas, quas scripserat, et dedit eis potestatem, ut salvi essent, et ut haberent pacem.

Et facta est haec scriptura in civitate Susis, et rex Ahasuerus, et Esther regina, et Judaei, qui erant in civitate, gaudebant, et celebrabant diem festum, et nuntii, qui veniebant, nuntiabant Judaeis, qui erant in ceteris civitatibus, ut haberent pacem.

Et fuere Judaei, qui erant in omnibus civitatibus regni Ahasueri, gaudentes, et celebrantes diem festum, et nuntii, qui veniebant, nuntiabant eis pacem.
