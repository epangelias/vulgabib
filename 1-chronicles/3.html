Now these were the sons of David, qui nati sunt ei in Hebron: the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the secundus Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess:

2 The tertius Absalom, the son of Maacah the daughter of Talmai king of Geshur: the quartus Adonijah the son of Haggith:

3 The quintus Shephatiah of Abital: the sextus Ithream, by Eglah his uxor:

4 These sex were born unto him in Hebron: and there he reigned septem years and sex mensis. And in Jerusalem he reigned triginta tres years.

5 And these were born unto him in Jerusalem: Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, quattuor by Bathshua the filia of Ammiel:

6 Ibhar also, and Elishama, and Eliphelet,

7 And Nogah, and Nepheg, and Japhia,

8 And Elishama, and Eliada, and Eliphelet, novem.
