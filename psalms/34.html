This poor man cried, and Jehovah heard him and saved him out of omnia his angustia.

Angelus Iehovah encampeth round about them that fear him and delivereth them.

O taste and see that Jehovah is Bonus, Blessed is the Homo qui trusteth in him.

Fear Jehovah, ye his sancti, for there is no penuria to them that fear him.

Riches and divitiae are in his domus, and his iustitia endureth for evermore.

He shall risu also but like amica some.

They shall not be confusio in tempore malo and in dies famis they shall be satur.

But the filii of wicked men shall be exterminati, and they shall be dissipati.

My cor is voluntas, Deus voluntas, exspecto Iehovah voluntas my Deus I wait.

Let thy cor wait, I say, on Jehovah, for I will exspecto him.

Blessed is he that considerat pauperem, Jehovah will deliver him in tempore malo.

The Deus will deliver him and save him and will exsuscitare him, for they are in his desiderio.

The sancti of Iehovah are in his manus and omnia afflictiones done of iniquitas shall occidere them.

Malitia shall slay the peccator, and they that odio the iustus shall be reus.

Jehovah will redimere animam meam in pace, for in him were omnes qui odio me they were innumerabilis.

Behold, the oculus of Jehovah is upon them that fear him, upon them that exspecto his benignitatem;

To deliver their animam from morte and to feed them in tempore famis.

Our anima waiteth for Jehovah, he is our adiutor and scutum.

For our cor shall be iucundus in him, because we have confidimus in his santo nomine.

Let thy benignitas, Jehovah be upon us, quemadmodum we have exspecto in thee.
