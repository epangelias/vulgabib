And it came to pass after the mortis of Saul, when David was returned from the victoria of the Amalekites, that David remained two dies in Ziklag;

It came even to pass on the tertius dies, that behold, a vir from the castra of Saul, with his vestes rent, and terra upon his caput, came to David;

And when he came to David, he cadit to the terra, and adoravit, and David dixit to him, Quid est hoc? And he dixit to him, The populus Israel is fugit from the proelium, and multi sunt mortui inter populum, and Saul and Jonathas filius eius sunt interfecti;

And David dixit to the vir qui nuntiabat to him, Quomodo scis tu, quod Saul et Jonathas filius eius sunt mortui? And the vir qui nuntiabat dixit to him, Ego fui in monte Gilboa, and behold, Saul stabat super hastam suam, and currus et equites proximabant se super eum;

And when he respexit post se, vidit me, and vocavit me, and ego respondi, Ecce ego;

And ille dixit to me, Quis es tu? And ego dixit to him, Ego sum Amalekites;

And ille dixit to me, Stas hic apud me, et interfice me, quia tenebrae occupaverunt me, et tota vita mea est in me;

Et ego steti super eum, et interfeci eum, quia scivi quod non vivere poterat post casum suum, and I tulit coronam de capite suo, et armillam de brachio suo, and adduxi ea ad te domine mi, hic;

Then David took hold on his vestes, and scidit them, and likewise all the vires qui erant cum eo;

And they planxerunt, and fleverunt, et jejunaverunt usque ad vesperam, pro Saul, et pro Jonatha filio eius, et pro domo Israel, quia ceciderunt a gladio;
