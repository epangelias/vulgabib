Leviticus 6   Back   Next

1 And this is the law of the holocaustum: This is the law of the victimae: it is Sanctus, therefore shall it be burnt by the filius of Aaron on the ara: it is Sanctus, it shall not be eaten.

2 Wheresoever the flammula is blown by the flamen, he that sacrificabit the holocaustum, even he and the filius of Aaron shall eat thereof in Sanctuario.

3 The vestis of the ministri sacerdotis shall be Sancta, for he sacrificabit the Sacrificium Domini.

4 Then the vestis which he putteth on shall be Vestimenta Sancta: the filius of the ministri sacerdotis shall lavabit them in Aqua Sancta and he shall indutus in ea.

5 The popina for the incendium and the mensa for the Panis Propositionis shall be ante the Tabernaculum.

6 Every filius of the ministri sacerdotis shall come to ministra: Vestitus with the vestes Sanctae and without ullae ornamenti, in ministerium shall eat thereof in Sanctuario.

7 If there be ulla pecunia with him in domo he shall die.

8 For in terra non licet edere ex his Sacrifiicis et nihil augeat et aedificabit cum iis: but of those Sacrficiorum aporteris erit terra sancta and comedes eos.

9 Whatsoever thou dost immolaberis ex terra Domini non sit communicatum ulli a posteritate sua and vide ne dicat Ibi simul sacrificaveris illas afferimus donum.
