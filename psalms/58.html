Psalms 58   Back   Next

Do ye indeed speak righteousness, o judices? do ye judge the people recte?

Yea, in corde ye work iniquitatem; ye weigh the violence of manus on terra.

The impiae are estranged from utero; they go astray from nativitate, speaking mendacium.

Their venenum is simile to the venenum of serpens; they are simile to the deaf asp that stoppeth aures

Which refuseth to hear the vox of the incantator, yea, the charm of the callidus serpens.

O Deus, contrite their dentes in os; break the brachia of the peccatores.

O Deus, how long shall the impiae, how long shall the impiae triumphare?

Quomodo they have destroyed populum tuum, and contrite the inheritance tuus.

Quomodo they have killed the orphan, and the vidua, and the peregrinus, qui is sine auxilio.

Et they have dixit, Videbit Dominus, et non videbit Deus Jacob.

O stulti, quando eritis docti? qui plantatis avenam, et metitis sinapi.

Qui expectatis vinum decimum, et non expectatis vinum totum?

Behold, o Deus, and deride them; O Dominus, quando tu derides eos, they are dissipati.

Quomodo they are brought to nihilum in momento; they are sublati, et non sunt; they perierunt quasi somnium cum evigilas.

O Deus, quando tu derides eos, they are dissipati; como ventus, et quasi nubes, quae perit.

Quasi radix in terra arida, quae non habet umorem; quia non habet radicem in terra.

Et they are dissipati, como pulvis, quem ventus dispergit; et como nubes, quae perit.

Tu es, Domine, spes mea; Tu es, Domine, refugium meum.

In Te, Domine, speravi; ne unquam confundar.
