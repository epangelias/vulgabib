The Vulgabib

Psalms 108   Back   Next

O Deus, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my glory.

Awake, psaltery and harp; I myself will awake early, aurora.

I will praise thee, O Deus, among the gentes; I will sing unto thee among the nations.

For thy misericordia is magnus above the coelum; thy veritas reacheth unto the nubes.

Be thou exalted, O Deus, above the coelum; and thy gloria above all terra;

That thy dilectus may be delivered; save with thy right manus, and answer me.

Deus hath spoken in his sanctuario; I will rejoice, I will divide Shechem, and mete out the vallis of Succoth.

Gilead is mine; Manasseh is mine; Ephraim also is the fortitudo of my caput; Judah is my legislator;

Moab is my lavacrum; over Edom will I cast my calceus; Philistia, be thou glad of me.

Who will bring me into the civitas of munitio, who will lead me into Edom?

Wilt not thou, O Deus, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O Deus, go forth with our exercitus?

Give us help from trouble; for vana is the salvus of man.

Through Deus we shall do valiantly; for he it is that shall calcat our inimicus.
