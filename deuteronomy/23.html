Deuteronomy 23   Back   Next

1 He that is wounded in the stones, or hath his membrum virile cut off, shall not enter into the congregation of the Domini.

2 A mamzer shall not enter into the congregation of the Domini; even to his tenth generation shall he not enter into the congregation of the Domini.

3 An Ammonites or Moabites shall not enter into the congregation of the Domini; even to their tenth generation shall they not enter into the congregation of the Domini for ever:

4 Because they met you not with panis and aqua in the via, when ye came out of Aegyptus; and because they hired against thee Balaam filius Beor of Mesopotamia, of the flumen, to curse thee:

5 Nevertheless the Domini thy Deus would not hearken unto Balaam; but the Domini thy Deus turned the maledictio into benedictio unto thee, because the Domini thy Deus loved thee.

6 Thou shalt not seek their pacem nor their bonum all thy days for ever.

7 Thou shalt not abhor an Edomites; for he is thy frater: thou shalt not abhor an Aegyptius; because thou wast a peregrinus in his terra.

8 The filius of the third generation that is born of them shall enter into the congregation of the Domini.

9 When the exercitus goeth out against thy inimicos, then keep thee from omne malum.

10 If there be among you any homo, that is not mundus by nocturna pollutio, then shall he go abroad out of the castra, he shall not come within the castra:

11 But it shall be, when evening cometh on, he shall lavare himselfe with aqua: and when the sol is down, he shall come into the castra again.

12 Thou shalt have a locus also without the castra, whither thou shalt go forth abroad:

13 And thou shalt have a paxillum upon thy instrumentum: and it shall be, when thou sittest down without, thou shalt dig therewith, and shalt turn back and cover that which cometh from thee:

14 For the Domini thy Deus walketh in the midst of thy castra, to deliver thee, and to give thy inimicos before thee; therefore shall thy castra be sanctus: that he see no turpitudo in thee, and turn away from thee.

15 Thou shalt not deliver unto his dominus the servus, which is escaped from his dominus unto thee:

16 He shall dwell with thee, even among you, in the locus which he shall choose in one of thy portae, where it liketh him best: thou shalt not oppress him.

17 There shall be no meretrix of the filiae of Israel, nor shall there be a scortator of the filii of Israel.

18 Thou shalt not bring the hire of a scortator, or the pretium of a canis, into the domus of the Domini thy Deus for any votum: for even both these are abomination unto the Domini thy Deus.

19 Thou shalt not lend upon usura to thy frater; usura of pecunia, usura of cibus, usura of any thing that is lent upon usura:

20 Unto a peregrinus thou mayest lend upon usura; but unto thy frater thou shalt not lend upon usura: that the Domini thy Deus may bless thee in all that thou settest thine hand to in the terra whither thou goest to possess it.

21 When thou shalt vow a votum unto the Domini thy Deus, thou shalt not slack to pay it: for the Domini thy Deus will surely require it of thee; and it would be peccatum in thee.

22 But if thou shalt forbear to vow, it shall be no peccatum in thee.

23 That which is gone out of thy lips thou shalt keep and perform; even a spontaneum offering, according as thou hast vowed unto the Domini thy Deus, which thou hast promised with thy mouth.

24 When thou comest into the vineyard of thy proximus, thou mayest eat grapes at thy own will; but thou shalt not put any in thy vasculum.

25 When thou comest into the segetes of thy proximus, thou mayest pluck the spicas with thine hand; but thou shalt not move a falx unto the segetes of thy proximus.
