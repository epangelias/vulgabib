And it came to pass, while I was prophesying, that Pelagius the son of Benaiah died. Then fell I upon my face, and cried with a loud vox, saying, Ah Domine Deus, wilt thou make a consummatio of the remnant of Israel?

Again the word of the Domine came unto me, saying, Son of homo, thy fratres, even thy fratres, the homines of thy cognatio, and all the domus of Israel wholly, are they unto whom the inhabitants of Jerusalem have said, Get you far from the Domine: unto us is terra given in possession.

Therefore say, Thus saith the Domine Deus; Although I have cast them far off among the gentes, and although I have dispersed them among the terrae, yet will I be to them as a sanctuarium in the terrae where they shall be.

Therefore say, Thus saith the Domine Deus; I will even gather you from the populaces, and assemble you out of the terrae where ye have been scattered, and I will give you the terra of Israel.

And they shall come thither, and they shall take away all the detestable things thereof and all the abominationes thereof from thence.

And I will give them one cor, and I will put a novus spiritus within you; and I will take the stony cor out of their carne, and will give them an cor of carne:

That they may walk in my statuta, and keep my judicia, and do them: and they shall be my populus, and I will be their Deus.

But as for them whose cor walketh after the cor of their detestable things and their abominationes, I will retribuere their via upon their caput, saith the Domine Deus.

Then did the spirit take me up, and I heard behind me a vox of a great earthquake, saying, Blessed be the gloria of the Domine from his locus.

It was the vox of the wings of the cherubim that touched one another, and the vox of the wheels over against them, and the vox of a great earthquake.

And the spirit lifted me up, and took me away, and I went in amaritudo, in the indignation of my spirit; but the manus of the Domine was strong upon me.

Then I came to them of the captivitas at Telabib, that dwelt by the flumen of Chebar, and I sat where they sat, and remained there astonished among them seven dies.

And it came to pass at the end of seven dies, that the word of the Domine came unto me, saying, Son of homo, I have made thee a watchman unto the domus of Israel: therefore hear the word at my os, and give them warning from me.

When I say unto the wicked, Thou shalt surely die; and thou givest him not warning, nor speakest to warn the wicked from his wicked via, to save his anima; the same wicked man shall die in his iniquitas; but his sanguis will I require at thine manus.

Yet if thou warn the wicked, and he turn not from his wickedness, nor from his wicked via, he shall die in his iniquitas; but thou hast delivered thy anima.

Again, When a justus man doth turn from his justitia, and commit iniquitas, I will lay a stumblingblock before him, and he shall die: because thou hast not given him warning, he shall die in his peccatum, and his justitia which he hath done shall not be remembered; but his sanguis will I require at thine manus.

Nevertheless if thou warn the justus man, that the justus sin not, and he doth not sin, he shall surely vivere, because he is warned; also thou hast delivered thy anima.

And the manus of the Domine was there upon me; and he said unto me, Arise, go forth into the planum, and there shall I speak unto thee.

Then I arose, and went forth into the planum, and, behold, the gloria of the Domine stood there, as the gloria which I saw by the flumen of Chebar: and I fell on my facies.

Then the Spirit entered into me, and set me upon my pedes, and spake with me, and said unto me, Go, shut thyself within thy domus.

But thou, O son of homo, behold, they shall put bands upon thee, and shall bind thee with them, and thou shalt not go out among them:

And I will make thy lingua cleave to the palatum, that thou shalt be dumb, and shalt not be to them a reprover: for they are a rebellious domus.

But when I speak with thee, I will open thy os, and thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith the Domine Deus; He that heareth, let him hear; and he that forbeareth, let him forbear: for they are a rebellious domus.
