And God spake all these verba, saying,

I am Dominus thy Deus, which have brought thee out of terra Egypt, out of domus servitutis.

Thou shalt not have other dei before me.

Thou shalt not make thyself sculptura, or similitudo of any thing that is in caelum above, or that is in terra beneath, or that is in aqua beneath the terra:

Thou shalt not adorare them, nor servire them: I am Dominus thy Deus, Deus zelotes, visiting iniquitatem patrum upon filii unto the tertiam and quartam generationem of them that hate me;

And shewing misericordiam unto multae generations of them that diligunt me, and keep my mandata.

Thou shalt not take nomen Domini Dei tui in vanum: for Dominus will not hold eum innocens that taketh nomen Domini Dei tui in vanum.

Remember dies sabbati, to keep it sanctum.

Sex dies thou shalt labor, and facere omnia opera tua:

But septimus dies is sabbatum of Dominus thy Deus: in eo thou shalt not facere opus, thou, nor filius tuus, nor filia tua, nor servus tuus, nor ancilla tua, nor iumentum tuum, nor peregrinus qui est intra portas tuae:

For in sex diebus Dominus made caelum and terram, mare, and omnia quae in eis sunt, and requievit in septimo die: wherefore Dominus blessed dies sabbati, and sanctified it.

Honora patrem tuum and matrem tuam: that thy dies may be longi super terram which Dominus thy Deus giveth thee.

Non occides.

Non moechaberis.

Non furtum facies.

Non loqueris contra proximum tuum falsum testimonium.

Non concupisces domum proximi tui, non concupisces uxorem proximi tui, non servum, non ancillam, non bovem, non asinum, neque omnia quae proximus tuus habet.
