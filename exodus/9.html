The Vulgabib

Exodus 9   Back   Next

1. Then the Lord said unto Moses, Go in unto Pharaoh, and tell him, Thus saith the Lord God of the Hebraei, Let my populus go, that they may servire me.

2. And if thou refuse to let them go, behold, I will smite all thy fines with ranarum, and thy populus, and thy domus, and in thy terrae shall be ranarum.

3. And the manus of the Lord was upon thy possessiones which are in the campo, upon the equi, and the asini, and the camelos, and the boves, and the pecora: a very grievous pustula.

4. And the Lord shall separare between the iumenta of Israel, and the iumenta of Aegypti: and there shall non erit nothing of all that pertinet to filiis Israel.

5. And the Lord appointed a certus dies, saying, To-morrow the Lord shall facere this word in terra.

6. And the Lord fecit it the next dies: and all the iumenta of Aegypti mortua sunt: but of the iumenta of filiis Israel non mortuus est quidquam.

7. And Pharaoh sent, and beheld, and non erat mortuum quidquam of the iumenta of filiis Israel: and the cor Pharaonis obduratus est, and he non dimisit populum.

8. And the Lord said unto Moses, and unto Aaron, Tollite manum plenam de cinere forni, and projiciet eam Moses in caelum coram Pharaone.

9. And erit in pulverem super totam terram Aegypti, and will be in ulcerem, and scabies, in hominibus, et in iumentis per totam terram Aegypti.

10. And they took cinerem de forno, and steterunt coram Pharaone, and Moses proiecit eum in caelum: and erat ulcerem, et scabies in hominibus, et in iumentis.

11. And non poterant stantes sacerdotes a facie ulceris, quia ulcerem erat in sacerdotibus, et in cunctis Aegyptiis.

12. And the Lord obdurauit cor Pharaonis, and non audivit eos, sicut locutus fuerat Dominus per manum Moses.
