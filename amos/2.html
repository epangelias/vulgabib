The Vulgabib

Amos 2   Back   Next

1 Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Moab, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because he burned the bones of the king of Edom into lime:

2 But I will send a fire upon Moab, and it shall devour the palaces of Kerioth: and Moab shall die with tumult, with shouting, and with the sound of a bucina.

3 And I will cut off the iudex from the midst thereof, and will slay all the principes thereof with him, saith the Lord.

4 Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Judah, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they have despised the legem of the Lord, and have not kept his praecepta, and their idola, which their patres have followed, have led them astray:

5 But I will send a fire upon Judah, and it shall devour the palaces of Jerusalem.

6 Thus saith the Lord; For three transgressions of Israel, and for four, I will not turn away the punishment thereof; because they sold the iustum for argentum, and the pauper for a pair of caligae:

7 That pant after the pulchritudo of a puella, and they take a pledge from the nudus, and they turn aside the way of the humilis: and a vir and his mater have violated the same puella, to profane my sanctuarium, and to pollute that which is sanctum unto me.

8 And they lay themselves down upon vestes taken in pledge, and beside every altare: and they drink the vinum of the damnum in the domus of their deus.

9 Yet destroyed I the Amorrhæum from before them, whose altitudo was like the altitudo of cedri, and he was strong as the quercus: and I destroyed his fructus from above, and his radixes from beneath.

10 Also I brought you up from terra Ægypti, and led you forty annus through desertum, to possess the terram of the Amorrhæum.

11 And I raised up of your filii for prophetæ, and of your iuvenes for Nazarites. Is it not thus, O filii Israel? saith the Lord.

12 But ye gave the Nazarites vinum to drink; and commanded the prophetæ, saying, Prophetare non.

13 Behold, I am pressed under you, as a quadriga is pressed that is full of faenum.

14 And flight shall perish from the swift, and the fortis shall not strengthen his robur, nor shall the bellator deliver himself:

15 He that handleth the arcum shall not stand; and he that is velox of pedibus shall not deliver himself: nor shall he that rideth the equus deliver himself.

16 And he that is robustus among the fortissimos shall flee away naked in that die, saith the Lord.
