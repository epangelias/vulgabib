The Emerald Tablet 1   Back   Next

Verily, verily, I say unto you, the Emerald Tablet is a sacred text.

It is a cryptic, ancient writing that holds the secrets of alchemy and the mysteries of the universe.

True, it is a mysterious text, but with the light of understanding, its secrets shall be revealed unto thee.

Know, O seeker of wisdom, that this tablet holds the power to transform and to transmute.

It is a key, a guide, a map to the hidden treasures of the universe.

Heed the words, dear student, and thou shalt unlock the secrets of the Emerald Tablet.
