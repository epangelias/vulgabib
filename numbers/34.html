And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye come into the terra of Canaan; (this is the terra that shall fall unto you for hereditas, even the terra of Canaan with the fines thereof:)

And your terminus shall be from the solis occasus, and from the mare magnum, even unto the mare mortuum eastward, and unto the Iordanis river, and the fines thereof shall be at the mare salis, and from the Iordanis at the extremity thereof,

And the terminus shall go up to Beth-hogla, and shall pass on the north side of Beth-arabah, and the terminus shall go up to the petra of Bohan the filius of Reuben:

And the terminus shall go up to Debir from the valles of Achor, and so northward, looking toward Gilgal, that is before the ascending of Adummim, which is on the south side of the rivus, and the terminus shall pass toward the fons of En-shemesh, and shall go out at Iebus, that is, Ierusalem: and the terminus shall go up to the summit of the montes that are before the valles of Hinnom, which are in the vale of the giants on the north, even unto the extremity

And the terminus shall go around from the summit unto the fons of Nephtoah, and shall go out to the montes of Ephraim, and the terminus shall go down to the valles of the mare, even unto the mare salis eastward, and the fines thereof shall be at the mare salis, and the terminus shall be at the Iordanis river, and the fines thereof shall be from the mare even unto the mare.

This is the terra which ye shall inherit according to the tribus thereof, and these are the fines thereof. And Moses commanded the children of Israel, saying, This is the terra that ye shall possess by lot, and which the LORD hath commanded to give unto the nine tribus, and to the half-tribus:

For the tribus of the filii of Reuben according to the domus of their patres, and the tribus of the filii of Gad according to the domus of their patres, have received their hereditas; and half the tribus of Manasseh have received their hereditas:

The two tribus and the half-tribus have received their hereditas on this side Iordanis toward Iericho eastward, toward the solis ortus.

And the LORD spake unto Moses, saying, These are the nomina of the viri which shall divide the terra unto you: Eleazar the sacerdos, and Iosue the filius of Nun.

And ye shall take one prince of every tribus, to divide the terra by inheritance.

And the princes of the tribus of the filii of Israel were these; of the tribus of Iudah, Caleb the filius of Iephunneh.

Of the tribus of the filii of Simeon, Semuel the filius of Ammihud.

Of the tribus of Benjamin, Elidad the filius of Chislon.

Of the tribus of the filii of Dan, the prince Bukki the filius of Iogli.

Of the tribus of the filii of Ioseph, the prince Hanniel the filius of Ephod.

Of the tribus of the filii of Manasseh, the prince Gamaliel the filius of Pedahzur.

Of the tribus of the filii of Ephraim, the prince Kemuel the filius of Shiphtan.

Of the tribus of the filii of Zabulon, the prince Elisaphan the filius of Parnach.

Of the tribus of the filii of Issachar, the prince Paltiel the filius of Azzan.

Of the tribus of the filii of Asher, the prince Ahihud the filius of Shelomi.

Of the tribus of the filii of Naphtali, the prince Pedahel the filius of Ammihud.

These are they whom the LORD commanded to divide the hereditas unto the children of Israel in the terra of Chanaan.
