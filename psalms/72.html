Psalms 72   Back   Next

1 Give the king thy judicium, O God, and thy justitia to the king's son.

2 He shall judicare thy people with justitia, and thy pauperes with judicium.

3 The montes shall bring pacem to the people, and the colles, in justitia.

4 He shall judicare the pauperes of the people, he shall salvare the filii of the pauper.

5 They shall timere thee with the sol, and before the luna, when generatio and generatio shall pass away.

6 He shall descend sicut pluvia upon the foenum, sicut stillae stillantes super terram.

7 In his dies shall justitia florere, and abundantia of pacem, till there be no luna.

8 And he shall dominare from mari to mari, and from flumen to the fines terrae.

9 Before him shall procidere the Aethiopes, and his inimici shall lingere terram.

10 The reges Tharsis and the insulae shall offer dona, the reges Arabum and Saba shall offer munera.

11 Yea, all reges shall procidere before him, all gentes shall servire him.

12 For he shall salvare the pauper from the potentatus, and the pauper who hath non auxilium.

13 He shall parcere the anima pauperis, and parcere the anima egeni, and salvare eos from the iniquitas of their manus.

14 He shall redimere their anima from fraus and violencia, and pretiosus shall be their sanguis in his oculis.

15 And he shall vivere, and dabitur ei de thesauro aurum Sabae, et orabitur pro eo semper, tota die benedicetur ei.

16 There shall be a spica in terra super montem, the fructus eius shall concutere sicut Libanus, and de civitate shall florere sicut faenum terrae.

17 His nomen shall be in aeternum, ante solem habebit nomen, et benedicentur in ipso omnes tribus terrae, omnes gentes beatificabunt eum.

18 Benedictus Dominus Deus, Deus Israel, qui solum facit mirabilia.

19 Et benedictum nomen gloriae eius in aeternum, et implebitur gloria eius tota terra. Amen. Amen.

20 Finis orationum David filii Jesse.
