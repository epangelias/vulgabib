And say unto them, Thus saith Dominus, Iosue: Lo, I will bring against you Omnium gentium, and I will take of them to be my sacerdotes and my ministri, and they shall offer to me oblationes.

And it shall be, when they shall enter in at portas domus, that thou shalt say unto them, Thus saith Dominus Deus: Ye have brought iniquitatem upon your heads, saying: Tolle, tolle, usque ad fundamentum.

Thus saith Dominus Deus: This citadel shall be laid waste, and this civitas shall be bereaved of her ornamenta, and the multitudo of Israel shall be humbled by their iniquitates.

They shall fall by gladius, by famem, and by pestilentiam: he that is in agro, shall die by gladium: and he that is in civitate, shall be devoured by famem, and by pestilentiam.

And ye shall know, quia I am Dominus, when their interfecti shall be in medio of you, et cadavera vestra erunt in circuitu civitatis, et in omnibus portis eius.

And the residue of you shall be saved from gladium, qui erunt dispersi per universas regiones terrae: and ye shall know, quia I am Dominus, qui percussi manu forti, et extendi brachium, et effudi iram meam super vos.

Thus saith Dominus Deus: Haec est Ierusalem, in medio gentium posui eam, et in circuitu eius terras.

Et dixi ei: Haec dicit Dominus Deus: O Ierusalem, haec dicit Dominus Deus: In hereditate domus Israel generatio tua et familia tua, et non in iustitia, et non in aequitate.

Propter iniquitates tuas, et scelera patrum tuorum, qui fuere ante te, dicit Dominus Deus, qui percussi manu forti, et extendi brachium, et effudi iram meam super vos.

Propterea, haec dicit Dominus Deus: Eritis in sordibus, et in sceleribus vestris in conspectu gentium, et in omnibus viis vestris cognoscentur iniquitates vestrae.
