And the word of the Dominus came unto me, saying, Son of hominem, put forth a riddle, and speak a parabola unto the domus of Israel;

Thus saith the Dominus Deus; A great aquila with great alas, longwinged, full of plumarum, which took a cedar,

He cropped off the summit of his rami, and carried it into the terra of Canaan; he set it in a civitas of negotiatores;

He took also of the semen of the terra, and planted it in a terra of sementem, he placed it by many aquae, he set it as a salix.

And it grew, and became a vine of great extent, and low of stature, whose rami turned toward him, and the radices thereof were under him: so it became a vine, and brought forth rami, and shot forth vitis.

But there was another great aquila with great alas, which seemed greater than the first, the which also had great plumarum: and, behold, this vine did bend her radices toward him, and shot forth her rami toward him, that he might water it by the poriones of her sementem.

It was planted in a good terra by many aquae, that it might bring forth rami, and that it might be a vine of excellent fructus: but she was a vine of vitis of very little value.

Thus saith the Dominus Deus; Shall it prosper? shall he not pull up the radices thereof, and cut off the fructus thereof, that it wither? it shall wither in all the leaves of her frondes; and no man shall pluck it out of his potens, nor plant it;

And it shall be, when thou shalt say, Why doth the vine wither? that I may say, As the Domini dixit; Because it was transplanted, wither it shall wither; every folium of her radix shall wither, and all the fructus of her labor shall wither, and it shall be no more a vine, nor bring forth rami.

Thus saith the Dominus Deus; Say now to the rebellious domus of Israel, Know ye not what these are? say, Behold, the king of Babylon came to Jerusalem, and took the king thereof, and the principes thereof, and led them with him to Babylon;

And he took of the semen of the regnum, and made a foedus with him, and took an oath of him: he also took the potentissimos of the terra:

That the regnum might be base, that it might not lift itself up, but that by keeping of his foedus it might stand.

But he rebelled against him in mittendo nuntios into Aegyptum, that they might give him equos and multus populus. Shall he prosper? shall he that doth such things escape? shall he break the foedus, and be delivered?

As I live, saith the Dominus Deus, surely in the place where the king dwelt that made him king, whose oath he despised, and whose foedus he brake, even with him in the midst of Babylon he shall die.

Neither shall Pharaon with his multus populus, nor the exercitus of the Aegyptii, which are come to set him on the thronus, by the exercitus of the Chaldeans.

Thus saith the Dominus Deus; I will also make the multus populus of great nations to rise up out of the aquilon, and they shall set against thee, and I will give thee to be devoured by them.

Thy people shall fall by the gladius in the terra, by the gladius of the exercitus: and they that are in the siege shall be consumed by the famis: and they that remain of thee shall be scattered into all the venti.

And ye shall know that I am the Dominus: for they shall not be able to stand before the exercitus, and the exercitus of the nations.

And as for thee, O prophet, thus saith the Dominus Deus; Say unto every avis of the caelum, and to every bestia of the terra, Assemble yourselves, and come; gather yourselves on every side to my victimam of sacrifice, that ye may eat flesh, and drink sanguinem.

Ye shall eat the carne of the potentissimos, and drink the sanguinem of the principes of the terra, of the ramos, and of the taurinos, of the arietes, and of all the boves of Bashan.

And ye shall eat fat until ye be full, and drink sanguinem until ye be drunken: and ye shall be satisfied at my victimam which I have sacrificed for you.

Thus ye shall be satiated at my mensa, and drink sanguinem until ye be drunken, and rise up to play, as in the dies of your festum: and ye shall be unto me a people of sacrificium, and ye shall know that I am the Dominus.

And it shall come to pass in that dies, that I will give unto Gog a place there of sepulchra in Israel, the valley of the peregrinatores on the orient of the mare: and it shall stop the way of the peregrinatores: and there shall they bury Gog and all his multus populus: and they shall call it the valley of Hamon-gog.

And seven menses shall the domus of Israel be burying of them, that they may cleansen the terra.

Yea, all the people of the terra shall bury them; and it shall be unto them a renown in the dies when I shall be glorificatus, saith the Dominus Deus.

And, behold, they shall give names of the loca by the nomine of them: the valley of Hamon-gog, the valley of the exercitus of Gog.

And, behold, every loco of my terra shall be cleansed: thus saith the Dominus Deus.

And it shall come to pass in that dies, that I will give unto Gog a place of sepulturae there of Israel, the valley of the itinerum on the orient side of the mare: that it may stop the way of the itinerum: and Gog and all his populus shall be buried there, and they shall call it the valley of the exercitus of Gog.

And the domus of Israel shall bury them, that the terra may be cleansed.
