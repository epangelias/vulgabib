Psalms 148   Back   Next

Praise ye the Dominus. Praise ye the Dominus from the coelum; praise him in the excelsis.

Praise ye him, all ye angeli eius; praise ye him, all ye virtutes eius.

Praise ye him, sol et luna; praise him, all ye stellae et lumen.

Praise him, ye coeli coelorum, and ye aquae that are above the coelum.

Let them praise the nomen Domini; for he commanded, and they were created.

He hath also established them for ever and ever; he hath made a decree which shall not pass.

Praise the Dominus from the terra, ye dracones, and all ye abyssus.

Ignis, and grandine, and nix, and ventus tempestas, that execute his verbum.

Montes, and omnes colles; lignum fructiferum, and omnes cedri.

Bestiae, and omnes pecora; reptilia, and volatilia pennis.

Reges terrae, and omnes populi; principes, and omnes iudices terrae.

Iuvenes, et virgines; senes, cum iunioribus.

Let them praise the nomen Domini; for his nomen only is exaltatum; his gloria is above terra, et coelum.

He hath exaltatum cornu salutis nostrae in domo suo, in domo David servi sui.

He hath sent redemptionem populo suo; he hath commandatum testamentum suum in aeternum; sanctum et terribile nomen eius.

Initium sapientiae, timor Domini; intellegentia bonorum, omnes qui faciunt eam; laudatio eius manet in saeculum saeculi.
