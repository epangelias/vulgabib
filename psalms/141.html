The Vulgabib

Psalms 141   Back   Next

Lord, I cry unto thee: make haste unto me; give ear unto my voice, when I cry unto thee.

Let my prayer be set forth before thee as incensum, and the lifting up of my hands as the evening sacrificium.

Set a watch, O Lord, before my os; keep the portam of my lips.

Incline not my cor to any malum thing, to practise wicked works with homines who work iniquitas: and let me not eat of their deliciae.

Let the Justus smite me, it shall be a gratia: and let him reprove me, it shall be as oleum on the caput; for yet my oratio shall be of their malitia.

Their judices are overthrown by the sides of the fossa, they shall hear my verba; for they are suavis.

Our bones are scattered at the os of the sepulchrum, as when one cutteth and cleaveth wood upon the terra.

But mine oculi are unto thee, O Deus, my Lord: in thee is my fiducia; leave not my anima destitute.

Keep me from the laqueus that they have laid for me, and the fossa of the operarii; hide me from the congregation of the malignant.

Let the Justi expectare in the porta of the sepulchrum, and let them rejoice in the veritas; let them sing and laudare thy misericordia and veritas; for thou hast magnified thy bonitas above the caelum.
