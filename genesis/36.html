Now these are the generations of Esau, qui est Edom.

Esau took his wives of the daughters of Canaan; Adah filia Elon the Hittite, and Aholibamah filia Anah filia Zibeon the Hivite;

And Bashemath filia Ishmael, sororem Nebajoth.

And Adah bore to Esau Eliphaz; and Bashemath bore Reuel;

And Aholibamah bore Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah: these are the filii of Esau, qui natus est ad Esau in terra Canaan.

And Esau took his wives, and his filii, and his filiae, and all the animae of his domus, and his greges, and all his pecus, and all his substantiam, quae conquisierat in terra Canaan; and went into terra altera from facies fratris sui Jacob.

For their substantia erat multa, ut non poterant habitare simul, et terra peregrinationis eorum non poterat eos capere propter greges eorum.

And there was a bellum between the pastores of Esau and the pastores of Jacob.

And Esau habitabat in monte Seir: Esau est Edom.

And these are the generations of Esau patris Edom in monte Seir:

These are the nomina filiorum Esau; Eliphaz filius Adah uxoris Esau, Reuel filius Bashemath uxoris Esau.

And the filii of Eliphaz erant Teman, Omar, Zepho, and Gatam, and Kenaz.

And Timna erat concubina Eliphaz filii Esau; and she bore to Eliphaz Amalek: these are the filii of Adah uxor Esau.

And these are the filii of Reuel; Nahath, and Zerah, Shammah, and Mizzah: these are the filii of Bashemath uxor Esau.

And these are the filii of Aholibamah filia Anah filia Zibeon, uxor Esau; and she bore to Esau Jeush, and Jaalam, and Korah.
