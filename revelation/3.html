Revelation 3   Back   Next

And unto the angel of the church in Sardis write; These things saith he that hath the septem spirits of Deus, and the seven stellae; I know thy works, that thou hast a nomine that thou livest, and art mortuus.

Be thou watchful, and strengthen the remnant that are ready to mori: for I have not found thy works perfect before Deus.

Remember therefore how thou hast received and heard, and hold fast, and repent. If therefore thou shalt not watch, I will come upon thee as a fur, and thou shalt not know what hora I will come upon thee.

Thou hast a few nomine in Sardis which have not defiled their vestimenta; and they shall walk with me in white: for they are worthy.

He that overcometh, the same shall be clothed in white vestimenta; and I will not blot out his nomine out of the liber of life, but I will confess his nomine before Pater and before his angeli.

He that hath an aurem, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the ecclesiae.

And to the angel of the church in Philadelphia write; These things saith he that is sanctus, he that is verus, he that hath the clavem of David, he that openeth, and no man shutteth; and shutteth, and no man openeth;

I know thy works: behold, I have set before thee an apertam porta, and no man can shut it: for thou hast a little potentia, and hast kept my verbum, and hast not denied my nomine.

Behold, I will make them of the synagogue of Satan, which say they are Judaei, and are not, but do lie; behold, I will make them to come and worship before thy pedes, and to know that I have loved thee.

Because thou hast kept the verbum of my patientia, I also will keep thee from the hora of temptation, which shall come upon all the orbis, to try them that dwell upon the terra.

Behold, I come quicunque. Hold that fast which thou hast, that no man take thy coronam.

Him that overcometh will I make a columna in the templum of my Deus, and he shall go no more out: and I will write upon him the nomine of my Deus, and the nomine of the civitas of my Deus, which is novus Jerusalem, which cometh down out of caelum from my Deus: and I will write upon him my novum nomine.
