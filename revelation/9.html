And the fifth angel sounded, and I saw a star fall from heaven unto the earth: and to him was given the clavis, and he opened the fovea of the abyss;

And there arose a fumus out of the fovea, as the fumus of a great fornax;

And out of the fovea there came locustae upon the earth: and unto them was given potestas, as the scorpiones of the terra have;

And it was commanded them that they should not hurt the herba of the earth, neither any viridis thing, neither any arbor: but only homines who have not the signaculum Dei in their frontes;

And to them it was given that they should not kill them, but that they should be tormented five menses: and their torment was as the torment of a scorpion, when he striketh a hominem;

And in those dies shall homines seek mors, and not find it: and they shall desire to mori, and mors shall flee from them;

And the shapes of the locustae were like unto horses prepared unto proelium; and on their capita were similitudines, as it were coronae similes auro;

And they had facies, as it were facies hominum: and they had capilli, as the capilli of mulieres: and their dentes were, as the dentes leonum;

And they had loricas, as the loricas of ferro: and the voces of their alae were, as the voces of currus, many equorum running to proelium;

And they had caudas, and aculei, as the aculei scorpionum: and in their caudas was their potestas to hurt homines five menses;

And they had a rex over them, who is the angel of the abyss, whose name in hebraicum is Abaddon, but in graecum he hath the name Apollyon, and in latin he hath the name Exterminans;

And the quintus angelus sounded, and I saw a stellam that had fallen from caelum unto the terra: and to him was given the clavis of the puteus of the abyss;

And he opened the puteus of the abyss; and there arose a fumus out of the puteus, as the fumus of a great fornax;

And the sol and the aer were darkened by the fumus of the puteus;

And out of the puteus came locustae, and unto them was given potestas, as the scorpiones of the terra have;

And it was commanded them that they should not hurt the herba of the terra, neither any viridis thing, neither any arbor: but only homines who have not the signaculum Dei in their frontes;

And to them it was given that they should not kill them, but that they should be tormented five menses: and their torment was, as the torment of a scorpion, when he striketh a hominem;

And in those dies shall homines seek mors, and not find it: and they shall desire to mori, and mors shall flee from them;

And the shapes of the locustae were, as it were, horses prepared unto proelium; and on their capita were similitudines, as it were coronae similes auro;

And they had facies, as it were facies hominum: and they had capilli, as the capilli of mulieres: and their dentes were, as the dentes leonum;

And they had loricas, as the loricas of ferro: and the voces of their alae were, as the voces of currus, many equorum running to proelium;

And they had caudas, and aculei, as the aculei scorpionum: and in their caudas was their potestas to hurt homines five menses;

And they had a rex over them, who is the angel of the abyss, whose name in hebraicum is Abaddon, but in graecum he hath the name Apollyon, and in latin he hath the name Exterminans;

One woe is past; and, behold, there come yet two woe after these things;

And the sextus angelus sounded, and I heard a vox, as it were, the vox of many aquae, and the vox of a great thunder;

And the vox which I heard, was, as it were, the vox of harpensorum, playing on their harpae;

And they sung, as it were, a novum canticum before the throne, and before the quattuor animalia, and the seniores: and no man could learn that canticum, but the centum quadraginta quattuor milia, who were redeemed from the terra;

These are they which were not defiled with mulieres: for they are virgines: these are they who follow the Agnum whithersoever he goeth: these were redeemed from among homines, being the primi fructus unto Deus, and unto the Agnum;

And in their mouth was found no mendacium: for they are without macula before the throne of Deus.
