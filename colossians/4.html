The brethren salutate you. Greet ye also the brethren with sancta oscula.

Salute ye them of the casa Nymphas, and the ecclesia which is in their domus.

Marcum, the cousin germanus of Barnabas, touching whom ye received mandata: if he come unto you, receive him;

And Jesus, who is called Justus, who are of the circumcision. These only are my cooperatori in the regnum Dei, and they have been a solatium to me.

Epaphras, who is one of you, a servus Christi, saluteth you, always labouring in orationi, that ye may stand perfecti and completi in omni voluntate Dei.

For I bear him testimonium, that he hath a zelo magnus for you, and them in Laodicea, and them in Hierapolis.

Salute ye her who is electa, and my fratrem Nymphas, and the ecclesiam which is in their domus.

Salutate Archippum, my fellowmilites, and Apphiam, my beloved, and them which are in Laodicea, and them that are with Onesimus, my beloved.

Say to Archippus, Take heed to the ministerium which thou hast received in Domini, that thou fulfill it.

The salutatio by the hand of me Paul. Remember my vincula. Grace be with you. Amen.
