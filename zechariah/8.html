And the angel of the Dominus spoke again unto me out of the caelum, saying, Arise, and go into the valles, and speak unto the prophetae of Israel, saying, Thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus, saying, I am turned by the misericordia of Jerusalem: thus saith the Dominus; and the civitas shall be inhabited again, and the templum shall be built in loco suo, and the templum shall flourish, and the civitas shall be amplificata round about: and thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus, yet again shall there be in this loco, which is desolatus, without homines and without animalia, and in the civitases round about, upon the collis, and in the valle, and in the meridies, and in the terra, saith the Dominus.

Thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus; Behold, I will save my populus from the terra of the orient, and from the terra of the occasus solis;

And I will bring them, and they shall dwell in the midst of Jerusalem: and they shall be my populus, and I will be their Deus, in veritate and in iustitia.

Thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus; Let your manus be strengthened, ye that hear in these dies these verba by the os of the prophetae, which were in the die when the fundamentum of the templum of the Dominus of the exercitus was laied, that the templum might be built.

For before these dies there was no merces for homines, nor for animalia, nor was there any pacem to him that went out or came in because of the afflictio: for I set all homines every one against his proximus.

But now I will not be unto the reliqui of this populus as in the priori dies, saith the Dominus of the exercitus.

For the semen shall be prosperous; the vine shall give her fructus, and the terra shall give her augmentum, and the caelum shall give his rorem, and I will cause the reliqui of this populus to possess all these benedictiones.

And it shall come to pass, that as ye were a maledictum among the gentes, O domus of Iudah, and domus of Israel; so will I save you, and ye shall be a benedictio: fear not, but let your manus be strengthened.

For thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus; As I thought to punish you, when your patres provoced me to ira, saith the Dominus of the exercitus, and I repented not:

So again have I thought in these dies to do well unto Jerusalem and to the domus of Iudah: fear ye not.

These are the dies of which ye shall speak, When the semen shall be prosperous, and the vine shall give her fructus, and the terra shall give her augmentum, and the caelum shall give his rorem, and I will cause the reliqui of this populus to possess all these benedictiones.

Yea, many gentes and the magni populi shall come to seek the Dominus of the exercitus in Jerusalem, and to pray before the faciem of the Dominus.

Thus saith the Dominus of the exercitus; In those dies it shall come to pass, that ten homines shall take hold out of all languages of the gentes, even shall take hold of the vestimentum of him that is a Iudaeus, saying, We will go with you: for we have heard that Deus is with you.

And the Dominus shall be King over all the terra: in that die shall there be one Dominus, and his nomen one.

All the terra shall be turned into the valle, and the valle of the montes shall be exalted, and the ruinae shall be made plane: and the populus shall see the salus of Deus.

Yea, upon the pilus of the equus also, and upon the currus, the belli shall be, and the bellicosus arcus, and the hasta, and all the belli instrumenta shall be, saith the Dominus of the exercitus.

And it shall come to pass in that die, saith the Dominus of the exercitus, that I will cut off the nominibus of the idola out of the terra, and they shall no more be mentioned: and I will cause the prophetae and the spiritus immundus to pass out of the terra.

And it shall come to pass, that when any shall yet prophesie, then his pater and his mater that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest falsitas in the nomen of the Dominus: and his pater and his mater that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesie.

And it shall come to pass in that die, that the prophetae shall be confusi, every one from his visio when he hath prophesied, neither shall they wear a vestimentum of pilus to deceive:

But the propheta shall say, I am no propheta, I am an agricola; for the terra is sold to me of my iuventus.

And one shall say unto him, What are these vulnera in thine manus? Then he shall answer, Those with which I was wounded in the domus of my dilectio.

Awake, O gladius, against my pastor, and against the vir, my socius, saith the Dominus of the exercitus: smite the pastor, and the oves shall be dispersed: and I will turn mine manus upon the parvi.

And it shall come to pass, saith the Dominus of the exercitus, that two partes in the terra shall be cut off, and perish; but the tertia pars shall be laft therin.

And I will bring the tertia pars through the ignem, and will refine them as argentum is refined, and will try them as aurum is tried: they shall call on my nomen, and I will hear them: I will say, It is my populus: and they shall say, The Dominus is my Deus.
