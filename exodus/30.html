And thou shalt make an altar to burn incense upon: of lignum, shalt thou make it.

A cubit shall be the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof; foursquare shall it be: and two cubits shall be the height thereof: the cornua thereof shall be of the same.

And thou shalt overlay it with aurum, pure and fine: the top thereof, and the sides thereof round about, and the cornua thereof; and thou shalt make unto it a coronam of aurum round about.

And two rings of aurum shalt thou make to it under the coronam of it, by the two corners thereof, upon the two sides of it shalt thou make it; and they shall be for places for the vectes to bear it withal.

And thou shalt make the vectes of lignum, and overlay them with aurum.

And thou shalt put it before the velum that is by the arca of the testimonium, before the propitiatorium that is over the testimonium, where I will meet with thee.

And Aaron shall burn thereon sweet incense every morning: when he dresseth the lumina, he shall burn incense upon it.

And when Aaron lighteth the lumina at even, he shall burn incense upon it, a perpetual incense before the Lord throughout your generations.

Ye shall offer no strange incense thereon, nor burnt sacrifice, nor oblation; neither shall ye pour thereon libamen.

And Aaron shall make an atonement upon the cornua of it once in a year with the blood of the sacrificium expiatorii; once in a year shall he make atonement upon it throughout your generations: it is most sanctum unto the Lord.

And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

When thou takest the sum of the children of Israel after their number, then shall they give every man a redemption of his anima unto the Lord, when thou numberest them; that there be no pestilencia among them, when thou numberest them.

This they shall give, every one that passeth among them that are numbered, half a siclus of argentum, after the siclus of the sanctum: the siclus is twenty oboli: an half siclus shall be the oblatio unto the Lord.

Every one that passeth among them that are numbered, from twenty years old and above, shall give an oblatio unto the Lord.

The rich shall not give more, and the pauper shall not give less than half a siclus, when they give an oblatio unto the Lord, to make an atonement for your animae.

And thou shalt take the argentum of the atonement of the children of Israel, and shalt appoint it for the ministerium of the tabernaculum of the congregation; that it may be a memoriale unto the children of Israel before the Lord, to make an atonement for your animae.

And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

Thou shalt also make a lavacrum of aes, and his base of aes, to wash withal: and thou shalt put it between the tabernaculum of the congregation and the altar, and thou shalt put water therein.

For Aaron and his filii shall wash their manus and pedes thereat:

When they go into the tabernaculum of the congregation, they shall wash with water, that they die not; or when they come near to the altar to minister, to offer oblationem and incense unto the Lord:

So they shall wash their manus and pedes, that they die not: and it shall be a statutum for ever to them, even to him and to his seed in their generation.

And the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

Moreover the Lord spake unto Moses, saying,

Take thou also unto thee principia of aromata, of myrrha pure and of cinamomum, and of calamus, and of cassia, and of lignum aloes, equal weight shall it be:

And thou shalt make it an oleum of unctionem, an oleum compound after the arte of the apothecarius: it shall be an holy unctionem.

And thou shalt anoint the tabernaculum of the congregation therewith, and the arca of the testimonium,

And the table and all his vessels, and the candelabrum and his vessels, and the altar of incense,

And the altar of holocaust, and all his vessels, and the lavacrum and his base.

And thou shalt sanctify them, that they may be most sanctum: whatsoever toucheth them shall be sanctum.

And thou shalt anoint Aaron and his filii, and consecrate them, that they may minister unto me in the sacerdotium:

And thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel, saying, This shall be an holy unctionem unto me throughout your generations.

Upon man's flesh shall it not be poured, neither shall ye make any other like it, after the composition of it: it is holy, and it shall be holy unto you.

Whosoever compoundeth any like it, or whosoever putteth any of it upon a stranger, shall even be cut off from his populus.

And the Lord said unto Moses, Take unto thee aromata, stacte, and onycha, and galbanum; these sweet aromata with pure myrrha: of each shall there be a like weight:

And thou shalt make it a powder of perfume after the arte of the apothecarius, tempered together, pure and sanctum:

And thou shalt beat some of it very small, and put of it before the testimonium in the tabernaculum of the congregation, where I will meet with thee: it shall be unto you most sanctum.

And as for the perfume which thou shalt make, ye shall not make to yourselves according to the composition thereof: it shall be unto thee holy for the Lord.

Whosoever maketh like unto that, to enjoy the odorem thereof, shall even be cut off from his populus.
