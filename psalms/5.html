Psalms 5   Back   Next

Give ear to my verba, O Domine, consider my clamor, and hearken unto my oratio, O thou Omnipotens.

Ponder my verba diligently, and my groan.

Thou art my Deus, O Domine, I will seek thee early.

My oratio shall prevent the aurora, and thou shalt hearken unto my vox.

For thou art not a Deus that hath delectare in malitia, neither shall the impious habitare with thee.

The stultus shall not stand in thy sight, and thou hatest all the operarii of iniquitas.

Thou shalt destroy all who dicunt mendacium, the Domine will abhor the sanguinarios and dolosus mens.

But as for me, in tua multa misericordia, I will introibo in thy domus, I will adorare in thy sanctuarium holy, in tua revereantia.

O Domine, lead me in tua iustitia because of my inimici, make thy via straightforward before my facies.

For there is non veritas in sua boca, their interiora are periitia, their os is sepulchrum aperiens.

Iudica illos, O Domine, according to their iustitia, and according to the multifaria mala of their cordis, O Domine, according to the opera of their manus iudica eos.

Perfidus shall not be stabiles in terra, and viri sanguinum shall not parare in conspectu oculorum tuorum.

Propter retribuere, Domine, benedicens eos qui placuerunt tibi, benedicens eos cum beneficiis donare digneris.

Quia tu Domine, benedixisti iustus, et placatus es in eo, quasi scutum ponens in voluntate.
