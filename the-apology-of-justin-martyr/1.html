Reasonable, therefore, shall I be in giving a reason of the faith which is in me, and of the hope which is founded upon the manifestation of the Christ, qui est Veritas, and of the promise of the life which is to come through Him, qui est Vita.

I shall, therefore, proceed to discuss this proposition, as it were, with those who have trusted in Christ, qui est Fides, and have been led to the knowledge of the truth, qui est Veritas, and the promise of the life which is to come.

The apostles, qui sunt Apostoli, and the followers of the apostles, qui sunt Discipuli, have handed down, and we have received, that our Lord, qui est Dominus, Jesus Christ, qui est Jesus, has appointed these, qui sunt ipsi, to be the pillars of the Church, qui est Ecclesia, which He has founded, qui est Fundamentum.

For, after His resurrection, He gave them this injunction, qui est Mandatum, saying, 'Going, therefore, teach all nations, qui sunt Gentes, baptizing them in the name of the Father, qui est Pater, and of the Son, qui est Filius, and of the Holy Ghost, qui est Spiritus Sanctus.'

Then, again, He gave them this further injunction, qui est Mandatum, saying, 'And, lo, I am with you alway, qui est Semper, even to the end of the world, qui est Mundus.'
