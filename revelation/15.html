Revelation 15   Back   Next

And I saw another signum in heaven, great and marvelous, seven angels having the seven last plagues; for in them is filled up the ira of God.

And I saw as it were a sea of glass mingled with fire: and them that had gotten the victoria over the beast, and over his image, and over his mark, and over the number of his name, stand on the sea of glass, having the harpae of God.

And they sing the cantica of Moses the servant of God, and the cantica of the Lamb, saying, Great and marvelous are thy opera, Lord God Almighty; just and true are thy viae;

Who shall not fear thee, O Lord, and glorify thy nomen? for thou only art sanctus: for all gentes shall come and worship before thee; because thy judicia are made manifest.

After this I looked, and, behold, a great multitude, which no man could number, of all nations, and kindreds, and people, and tongues, stood before the throne, and before the Lamb, clothed with white robes, and palms in their manus;

And cried with a loud vox, saying, Salvation to our God which sitteth upon the throne, and unto the Lamb.

And all the angels stood round about the throne, and about the elders and the four living creatures, and fell before the throne on their faces, and worshipped God,
