Psalms 13   Back   Next

How long, O Deus, wilt thou forget me for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy faciem from me?

How long shall I take counsel in my animus, having sorrow in my cor in the day?

How long shall mine inimicus be exalted over me?

Consider and hear me, O Deus: lighten mine oculi, lest I sleep the somnus of morte;

Lest mine inimicus say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved;

But I have trusted in thy misericordia; my cor shall rejoice in thy salvus:

I will sing unto Deus, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.
