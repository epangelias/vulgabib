And it came to pass, as he went to Jerusalem, that he passed through the midst of Samaria and Galilaea. Et videns aliquid, coepit dicens: Quae est haec generatio?

They are like children sitting in the marketplace, and calling one to another, and saying, We have piped unto you, and ye have not danced; we have mourned to you, and ye have not lamented. Sunt sicut pueri sedentes in foro, et vocantes invicem, et dicentes: Canticum cecinimus vobis, et non saltastis; lamentavimus, et non planxistis.

The Son of man is come eating and drinking; and ye say, Behold a glutto and a bibo, a friend of publicanos and peccatores. Filius hominis venit manducans et bibens; et dicitis: Ecce gula et potator vini, amicus publicanorum et peccatorum.

And the Lord said, Whereunto then shall I liken the homines of this generation? Et dixit Dominus: Similes sunt cui comparabo homines generationis huius?

The earth bringeth forth fructus of herself; first the herba, then the calathus, after that the plena spica in the calathus. Terra fructum affert ipso, prius herbam, deinde spicam, novissime plenum granum in spica.

He spake also this parabolam; A certain homo planted a ficus tree in his vineyard; and he came and sought fructus thereof, and found none. Dixit autem et aliam parabolam: Quercus ficus plantavit homo in vinea sua; et venit quaerere in ea fructum, et non invenit.

Then said he unto the custos of the vineyard, Behold, these three anni I come seeking fructus on this ficus tree, and find none: cut it down; why cumbereth it the terra? Tunc ait ad cultorem vineae: Ecce anni tres hunc venio quaerens fructum in ficulnea hac, et non invenio; excide illam; quid et terra occupat?

And he answering said unto him, Lord, let it alone this year also, till I shall dig about it, and dung it: And if it bear fructus, well: but if not, then after that thou shalt cut it down. Et respondens custos vineae, dixit illi: Domine, dimitte illam et hoc anno, donec circumfodiam et stercorabo; et si fructus fecerit, bene; alias in sequenti demoliar eam.

Then drew near unto him all the populus to hear him, and the scribae and Pharisaei, to reprehend him. Et accesserat ad eum omnis plebs audire eum, et scribae et Pharisaei, ut eum reprehenderent.

And he said, Also to you, which are the reliquiae of Israel, to whom the verbum of God came, saying: This people honoureth me with the labiis, but their cor is far from me. Dixit autem et ad eos, qui relicti erant ex Israhel, ad quos verbum Dei pervenerat, dicens: Populus hic labiis me honorat; cor autem eorum longe est a me.

The Spiritus of the Lord is upon me, because he hath anointed me to evangelizare pauperibus; he hath sent me to heal contritos corde, to praedicare captivis remissionem, et caecis visum, to set at libertatem oppressos, To praedicare annum Domini acceptum. Spiritus Domini super me, eo quod unxerit me, evangelizare pauperibus misit me, sanare contritos corde, praedicare captivis remissionem, et caecis visum, dimittere confractos in remissionem, praedicare annum Domini acceptum.
