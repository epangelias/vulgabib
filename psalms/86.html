Psalms 86   Back   Next

1 Bow down thine aures, O Domine, and hearken to my vox, for I am miser and pauper.

2 Preserve my anima, for I am sanctus: O thou, Deus, save thy servus that trusteth in te.

3 Be gratia unto me, O Domine, for I cry unto thee daily.

4 Rejoice the cor of thy servus, for unto thee, O Domine, do I lift up my anima.

5 For thou, Domine, art bonus and benignus, and of great misericordia unto all them that invocant te.

6 Give aures, O Domine, unto my oratio, and attend unto the vox of my deprecatio.

7 In the dies of my tribulatio I will invocare thee, for thou, Domine, hast redemptus me.

8 There is non inter omnes dei, O Domine, like unto thee, nec opera similia tuis.

9 Omnes gentes, quascumque fecisti, venient and adore they faciem, Domine, and glorificabunt nomen tuum.

10 Quoniam tu magnus es, et faciens mirabilia: tu es Deus solus.

11 Doce me, Domine, viam tuam, and I will ambulare in veritate tua: unire my cor, ut timeam nomen tuum.

12 I will gratias, Domine, Deus meus, in toto corde meo, and I will honor nomen tuum in aeternum.

13 Quoniam grande est misericordia tua ad me, and salvasti animam meam ex inferno inferiori.

14 O Domine, superbi sunt adversus me, and congregati sunt inimici mei: they have non intellectum, nec requirunt te.

15 Et tu, Domine, Deus misericors and benignus, patiens et multae misericordiae et veritas.

16 Osteriza me, Domine, and exaudi orationem meam: give aures, Domine, and exaudi orationem meam.

17 In the dies, in qua invoco te, exaudi me velociter: da spiritum meum, Domine, and non avertas faciem tuam a me.

17 O Domine, non confundas me: custodi me, Domine, quoniam in te speravi.
