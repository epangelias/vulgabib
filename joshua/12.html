Joshua 12   Back   Next

Now these are the kings of the terra who reigned in the land of Canaan, whom the children of Israel smote, and possessed their terra on the other side Iordanem by the great sea, from Baalgad in the valley of Libanus even unto the mount Halak, that goeth up to Seir; which Iosue gave unto the tribes of Israel for a possession according to their divisions;

One king of Iericho, one, the king of Hai, which is beside Bethel, one,

The king of Ierusalem, one, the king of Hebron, one,

The king of Iarmuth, one, the king of Lachis, one,

The king of Eglon, one, the king of Gezer, one,

The king of Debir, one, the king of Geder, one,

The king of Horma, one, the king of Arad, one,

The king of Libna, one, the king of Adullam, one,

The king of Makkedah, one, the king of Bethel, one,

The king of Tappuah, one, the king of Hepher, one,

The king of Aphek, one, the king of Lassaron, one,

The king of Madon, one, the king of Hazor, one,

The king of Simron, one, the king of Achshaph, one,

The king of Taanach, one, the king of Megiddo, one,

The king of Kadesh, one, the king of Iokneam of Carmel, one,

The king of Dor in the coast of them that remaineth of the kindreds of Dor, who are in Iezreel, one,

The king of the nations of Gilgal, one,

The king of Tirzah, one: all the kings thirty and one.
